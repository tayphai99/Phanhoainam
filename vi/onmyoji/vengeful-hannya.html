Vengeful Hannya

Kỹ năng 1: Returning Hatred
Sát Thương Đặc Biệt Lửa: 0

Attabks 1 enemy, dealing damage equal to 100% of his ATK.
If Sealing Field is on, it inflicts a 2-strike attack instead, dealing damage equal to 80% of his ATK and inflicting a 15% HP Steal for each strike.
(Hiệu Quả Duy Nhất) Gains a layer of Hatred Mark.

  • Lv.2: Tăng sát thương lên 105% or to 84% for each strike if the field is on.
  • Lv.3: Tăng sát thương lên 110% or to 88% for each strike if the field is on.
  • Lv.4: Tăng sát thương lên 115% or to 92% for each strike if the field is on.
  • Lv.5: Tăng sát thương lên 125% or to 100% for each strike if the field is on.

Hatred Mark
[General, Mark] Vengeful Hannya's special mechanic. Stacks up to 9 layers. When stacked the maximum number of times, removes all controlling effects on him and raises his Move Bar by 35%.
When Sealing Field is active, he cannot gain Hatred Marks.

Hiệu Quả Duy Nhất
Nếu có nhiều thức thần cùng tên trong đội hình của bạn, chỉ một trong các kỹ năng của thức thần đó sẽ có hiệu lực.

Kỹ năng 2: Deep-rooted Grudge
Đặc Biệt Tăng Tốc Lửa: 3 Hiệu Quả Duy Nhất

Whenever an enemy inflicts critical damage, has a 40% chance of gaining a layer of Hatred Mark. Triggers only once per action taken.
Using the skill costs 9 layers of Hatred Mark. Creates a Sealing Field with 9 demon masks to shadow all enemies. The field closes when all demon masks are shattered, with a 100% Tỷ Lệ Cơ Bản of inflicting a Spreading Hatred lasting 1 turn on all enemies.

  • Lv.2: When a non-summoned entity ally is KO'd, gain 3 layer(s) of Hatred Mark.
  • Lv.3: Reduces orbs required by 3.
  • Lv.4: Increases chance of triggering to 80%.
  • Lv.5: Upper Hand: Gains 6 layers of Hatred Mark.

Hatred Mark
[General, Mark] Vengeful Hannya's special mechanic. Stacks up to 9 layers. When stacked the maximum number of times, removes all controlling effects on him and raises his Move Bar by 35%.
When Sealing Field is active, he cannot gain Hatred Marks.

Spreading Hatred
[Debuff, Status] Increases Crit by 50% and reduces damage by 35%

Hiệu Quả Duy Nhất
Nếu có nhiều thức thần cùng tên trong đội hình của bạn, chỉ một trong các kỹ năng của thức thần đó sẽ có hiệu lực.

Sealing Field
Substitutes the passive skills of all enemies to Grudge Forsaken and their Soul Set effects to Vengeful Demon. The passive skill and Soul Set effects they originally have are deemed as Suppressed and Sealed. Monsters are immune to the skill.
When the field is on, Vengeful Hannya has 30 increased SPD. Each non-summoned entity enemy shatters a demon mask when their turn ends.

Tỷ Lệ Cơ Bản
Tỷ lệ bị ảnh hưởng bởi Hiệu Ứng Chính Xác (Effect HIT).

Vengeful Demon
Reduces Crit by 15%.
For each orb spent, loses HP by 7% of current HP (to a max of 145% of Vengeful Hannya's ATK).

Lose
Different from taking damage, losing HP does not lands a Crit, cannot be absorbed by shields, and does not trigger effects that would trigger for taking damage.

Suppress
Prevents shikigami passive skills from taking effect.

Seal
Prevents the effects of shikigami's souls.

Upper Hand
Take action at the start of battle.

Grudge Forsaken Đặc Biệt Lửa: 1
The enemy can actively use this skill to shatter 2 demon masks to close the Sealing Field early.

Kỹ năng 3: Promised Wish
Sát Thương Lửa: 3

Inflicts a 3-strike attack on 1 enemy, dealing damage equal to 72% of his ATK. It then inflicts another attack (plus 1 if Sealing Field is on) on all enemies, dealing damage equal to 72% for each attack.
(Hiệu Quả Duy Nhất) Gains 3 layers of Hatred Mark.

  • Lv.2: Tăng sát thương lên 74%.
  • Lv.3: Tăng sát thương lên 77%.
  • Lv.4: Tăng sát thương lên 80%.
  • Lv.5: For each non-summoned entity ally with HP lower than 50%, an attack is inflicted on a random enemy, dealing damage equal to 80% of his ATK.

Hatred Mark
[General, Mark] Vengeful Hannya's special mechanic. Stacks up to 9 layers. When stacked the maximum number of times, removes all controlling effects on him and raises his Move Bar by 35%.
When Sealing Field is active, he cannot gain Hatred Marks.
