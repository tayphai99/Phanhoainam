Hakuzosu

Kỹ năng 1: Flamed Shadow
Sát Thương Hiệu Ứng Bất Lợi Lửa: 0

Deals damage equal to 100% of his ATK with a 100% Tỷ Lệ Cơ Bản of inflicting Burnt Flame lasting 1 turns on the enemy.

  • Lv.2: Tăng sát thương lên 105%.
  • Lv.3: Tăng sát thương lên 110%.
  • Lv.4: Tăng sát thương lên 115%.
  • Lv.5: Tăng sát thương lên 125%.

Burnt Flame
[Debuff, Status] Reduces ATK by 10%.

Tỷ Lệ Cơ Bản
Tỷ lệ bị ảnh hưởng bởi Hiệu Ứng Chính Xác (Effect HIT).

Kỹ năng 2: Sheltering Pact
Hỗ Trợ Phòng Thủ Hiệu Ứng Bất Lợi Bị Động Hiệu Quả Duy Nhất

At the end of his turn, grants Fox Shadow: Shelter to each of his allies lasting until his next turn.

  • Lv.2: Increases Fox Shadow: Shelter's damage absorbed to 30%.
  • Lv.3: When a Fox Shadow: Shelter is broken, Hakuzosu launches a counter-attack against the source of the damage if he's not under any controlling effect, dealing damage equal to 10% of his max HP, and has a 100% base chance of reducing their Crit by 30% for 1 turn (the effect cannot be stacked). His damage is not increased if multiple Fox Shadow: Shelters are broken at the same time.
  • Lv.4: Increases Fox Shadow: Shelter's damage absorbed to 40%.
  • Lv.5: Increases his SPD by 60 and Effect RES by 60% indefinitely. Each Fox Shadow: Shelter an ally has reduces his SPD boost from the effect by 10 and Effect RES boost by 10%.

Fox Shadow: Shelter
[Buff, Mark] When taking damage, absorb 20% of the damage to a max of 10% of Hakuzosu's max HP.

Hiệu Quả Duy Nhất
Nếu có nhiều thức thần cùng tên trong đội hình của bạn, chỉ một trong các kỹ năng của thức thần đó sẽ có hiệu lực.

Kỹ năng 3: Shadowing Shelter
Hỗ Trợ Phòng Thủ Lửa: 3

Creates a Fox Shadow field that lasts 1 turn. Whenever an ally takes single-target damage or Indirect Damage, the damage is reduced by 35%.

  • Lv.2: Reduces damage to 40%.
  • Lv.3: Reduces damage to 45%.
  • Lv.4: Reduces damage to 50%.
  • Lv.5: Increases duration to 2 turns.

Indirect Damage
Damage type. Does not trigger soul effects, cannot be shared, and is guaranteed to land critical hits on enemies with 0 DEF.
