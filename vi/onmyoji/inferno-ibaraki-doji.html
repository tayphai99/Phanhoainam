Inferno Ibaraki Doji

Kỹ năng 1: Demonic Claw
Sát Thương Lửa: 0

Slashes his demonic claw at 1 enemy, dealing damage equal to 100% of his ATK.

  • Lv.2: Tăng sát thương lên 105%.
  • Lv.3: Tăng sát thương lên 110%.
  • Lv.4: Tăng sát thương lên 115%.
  • Lv.5: Tăng sát thương lên 125%.

Kỹ năng 2: Enraged Will
Sát Thương Bị Động

When the demonic flame from Inferno Gate would attack more than 1 enemy, for each extra enemy, increases the damage of the demonic flame by 10% to a max of 20%.

  • Lv.2: Increases the extra damage inflicted to a max of 30%.
  • Lv.3: Increases the extra damage inflicted to a max of 40%.
  • Lv.4: Increases the extra damage inflicted to a max of 50%.
  • Lv.5: If the demonic flame KO's a non-summoned enemy entity, the Demonic Arm attack that follows inflicts an extra attack, dealing damage equal to the total excess damage from the previous demonic flame.

Kỹ năng 3: Inferno Gate
Sát Thương Lửa: 4

Channels strong demonic power to open the Inferno Gate and commands the burning demonic flame to attack all enemies, dealing damage equal to 131% of his ATK. Also calls forth a giant demonic arm to crush 1 enemy, dealing damage equal to 131% of his ATK.
If the target is KO'd by the demonic flame, the Demonic Arm attacks the enemy with the highest HP.

  • Lv.2: Increases damage from demonic flame and demonic arm to 138%.
  • Lv.3: Increases damage from demonic flame and demonic arm to 145%.
  • Lv.4: Increases damage from demonic flame and demonic arm to 151%.
  • Lv.5: Increases damage from demonic flame and demonic arm to 164%.
