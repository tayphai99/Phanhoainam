Azurestorm Ichimokuren

Kỹ năng 1: Wind Amulet: Destroy
Sát Thương Lá Chắn Lửa: 0

Attacks 1 enemy with his wind dragon power, dealing damage equal to 80% of his ATK, and creates a Wind Shield to protect himself that lasts 3 turns, absorbing damage equal to 60% of his ATK.
If he already has a Wind Shield, creates a Wind Shield to protect an unprotected ally with the lowest proportion of HP.

  • Lv.2: Tăng sát thương lên 85%.
  • Lv.3: Tăng sát thương lên 90%.
  • Lv.4: Tăng sát thương lên 95%.
  • Lv.5: Tăng sát thương lên 100%.

Wind Shield
Shield created by Azurestorm Ichimokuren that absorbs damage (maxed at 200% of target's HP) and cannot be dispelled. Each Wind Shield increases the shield creator's SPD by 30. When a Wind Shield fails, raises the Move Bar of the shield creator by 12%.

Kỹ năng 2: Wind Amulet: Shield
Lá Chắn Tăng Tốc Lửa: 2

At the end of each action (of both allies and enemies), if he's not under any Controlling Effect, creates a Wind Shield to protect an ally with HP below 30%, absorbing damage equal to 188% of his ATK. CD: 3 turns.
Active skill: Creates two Wind Shields, each lasting 3 turns, to protect the target ally and the ally with the lowest proportion of HP who doesn't have a Wind Shield on them, absorbing damage equal to 188% of his ATK.

  • Lv.2: Increases damage absorbed by Wind Shield to 198%.
  • Lv.3: Increases damage absorbed by Wind Shield to 207%.
  • Lv.4: Increases damage absorbed by Wind Shield to 217%.
  • Lv.5: Reduces cooldown duration to 2 turns.

Hiệu Ứng Kiểm Soát
Freeze, Sleep, Confuse, Taunt, Silence, Bound, Morph, Daze, Provocation, Deep Freeze, Shadowbound, and Frostbound are controlling effects. The last six cannot be dispelled. Frostbound cannot be removed.

Wind Shield
Shield created by Azurestorm Ichimokuren that absorbs damage (maxed at 200% of target's HP) and cannot be dispelled. Each Wind Shield increases the shield creator's SPD by 30. When a Wind Shield fails, raises the Move Bar of the shield creator by 12%.

Kỹ năng 3: Wind Halted: Dragonfall
Sát Thương Đặc Biệt Lửa: 3

Dragon soars into the clouds and calls forth a storm to attack all enemies, dealing damage equal to 158% of his ATK. If the damage causes the HP of a target to drop below 80%, has a 100% Tỷ Lệ Cơ Bản of inflicting Isolation lasting 1 turn on them.
Consumes the Wind Shields on all allies. For each one consumed, inflicts damage equal to 158% of his ATK on a random enemy, and has a 100% Tỷ Lệ Cơ Bản chance of inflicting Dragon Fury lasting 2 turns on them.

  • Lv.2: Increases both damage amounts to 166%.
  • Lv.3: Increases both damage amounts to 174%.
  • Lv.4: Increases both damage amounts to 182%.
  • Lv.5: When a Wind Shield is consumed, Recoveres the HP of the ally who originally had one of them by the remaining HP of the shield that was on them.

Dragon Fury
[Debuff, Mark] Using skills costs an extra 2 orbs. Ineffective on monsters.

Tỷ Lệ Cơ Bản
Tỷ lệ bị ảnh hưởng bởi Hiệu Ứng Chính Xác (Effect HIT).

Isolation
Damage taken cannot be shared or redirected by other effects, including Hakuzosu's skill Shadowing Shelter, Shouzu's skill Water Circuit and Soul Edge's effect among others.
