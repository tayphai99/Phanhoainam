Fukengaku

Kỹ năng 1: Heavy Boulder
Sát Thương Lửa: 0

Harnesses a giant boulder with the power of the mountain god and smashes it at 1 enemy, dealing damage equal to 100% of his ATK.

  • Lv.2: Tăng sát thương lên 105%
  • Lv.3: Tăng sát thương lên 110%
  • Lv.4: Tăng sát thương lên 115%
  • Lv.5: Tăng sát thương lên 125%, increases own Effect RES by 50% for 2 turns after using the skill.

Kỹ năng 2: Mountain Hiking
Đặc Biệt Lửa: 3 Hiệu Quả Duy Nhất

Creates a Hiking Field that lasts 2 turns. When an ally takes critical damage, if the damage dealer has a higher Crit DMG, reduces the damage by 0.08% for each 1% Crit DMG difference (max 1,200% of Fukengaku's starting DEF reduced). After the field is active, allies gain a Rock Pattern: Cloud's Color on their first turn. Afterwards, if an ally uses the same skill they used on their last turn, they gain a Rock Pattern: Cloud's Color; if an ally uses a skill different from the one they used on their last turn or they're under Controlling Effects, they gain a Rock Pattern: Mountain's View at the end of the turn.

  • Lv.2: When an ally is attacked while the Hiking Field is active, if the attacker's Crit DMG is higher than 3 times the amount of Fukengaku's Crit DMG, reduces their Crit by 50% for the turn.
  • Lv.3: Increases the duration of Hiking Field to 3 turns.
  • Lv.4: While the field is active, the ATK of all allies is increased by 50% of Fukengaku's starting DEF (max 20% of the allies' respective starting ATK increased.)
  • Lv.5: Upper Hand: Uses Mountain Hiking.

Rock Pattern: Cloud's Color
[Buff, Status] Increases ATK by 200% of Fukengaku's starting DEF (max 40% of own starting ATK increased) for 1 turn.

Rock Pattern: Mountain's View
[Buff, Status] A shield that absorbs damage equal to 350% of Fukengaku's starting DEF and increases Effect RES by 30% for I turn.

Hiệu Quả Duy Nhất
Nếu có nhiều thức thần cùng tên trong đội hình của bạn, chỉ một trong các kỹ năng của thức thần đó sẽ có hiệu lực.

Hiệu Ứng Kiểm Soát
Freeze, Sleep, Confuse, Taunt, Silence, Bound, Morph, Daze, Provocation, Deep Freeze, Shadowbound, and Frostbound are controlling effects. The last six cannot be dispelled. Frostbound cannot be removed.

Upper Hand
Take action at the start of battle.

Kỹ năng 3: God of Mountain
Chữa Trị Xua Tan Hỗ Trợ Bị Động Hiệu Quả Duy Nhất

While the Hiking Field is active, whenever an ally gains a Rock Pattern: Cloud's Color. stack, he gains a Rock Pattern: Cloud stack; and whenever an ally gains a Rock Pattern: Mountain's View. stack, he gains a Rock Pattern: Mountain stack.

  • Lv.2: At the start of his turn, randomly gains a Rock Pattern: Cloud stack or a Rock Pattern: Mountain stack.
  • Lv.3: Each 1 Rock Pattern: Mountain stack consumed dispels 1 debuff or 1 Controlling Effect from a random ally, prioritizing on Controlling Effects.
  • Lv.4: Upper Hand: Gains a Rock Pattern: Cloud stack and a Rock Pattern: Mountain stack.
  • Lv.5: Extend the duration of non-critical damage increased to 2 turns.

Rock Pattern: Cloud's Color
[Buff, Status] Increases ATK by 200% of Fukengaku's starting DEF (max 40% of own starting ATK increased) for 1 turn.

Rock Pattern: Cloud
[General, Mark] Max 5 stacks. Using normal attack consumes all stacks. Each stack consumed increases all allies' non-critical damage dealt by 3% for 1 turn. Max 15% increase (max 6,000% of Fukengaku's starting DEF increase). Multiple triggers causes the effect to stack and refreshes the duration.

Rock Pattern: Mountain's View
[Buff, Status] A shield that absorbs damage equal to 350% of Fukengaku's starting DEF and increases Effect RES by 30% for I turn.

Rock Pattern: Mountain
[Genera|, Mark] Max 5 stacks. Using normal attack consumes all stacks. Each stack heals the HP of all allies by 100% of Fukengaku's starting DEF.

Hiệu Quả Duy Nhất
Nếu có nhiều thức thần cùng tên trong đội hình của bạn, chỉ một trong các kỹ năng của thức thần đó sẽ có hiệu lực.

Upper Hand
Take action at the start of battle.
