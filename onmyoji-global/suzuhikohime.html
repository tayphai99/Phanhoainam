Suzuhiko hime

Kỹ năng 1: Shake Bell
Sát Thương Lửa: 0

Wields her chiming blade and sends fiery flames to attack 1 enemy, dealing damage equal to 100% of her ATK

  • Lv.2: Tăng sát thương lên 105%
  • Lv.3: Tăng sát thương lên 110%
  • Lv.4: Tăng sát thương lên 115%
  • Lv.5: Tăng sát thương lên 125%

Kỹ năng 2: Scorching Fire
Sát Thương Chữa Trị Lá Chắn Lửa: 0 Hiệu Quả Duy Nhất

Suzuhikohime cannot be revived when she's KO'd. When her HP drops below 50% of her max HP, she permanently gains Holy Fire.
Active skill: Resets the cooldown of Holy Fire, recovers her HP by 40% of her max HP, and Recovers the HP of other allies by 12% of her max HP.

  • Lv.2: Increases the HP required to gain Holy Fire to 75%
  • Lv.3: If Suzuhikohime has Holy Fire, at the end of a non-summoned ally entity's turn, recovers her HP by 6% of her max HP.
  • Lv.4: When taking lethal damage, changes the skill to Undying Flame.
  • Lv.5: When she gains Holy Fire, creates a shield that cannot be dispelled and lasts 2 turns, absorbing damage equal to 120% of her ATK.

Holy Fire
[Buff, Mark] When her HP reaches her max HP and she's not under controlling effects or Banished, she immediately uses Fire Rite free of orb cost. The effect has a cooldown for 2 turns.

Hiệu Quả Duy Nhất
Nếu có nhiều thức thần cùng tên trong đội hình của bạn, chỉ một trong các kỹ năng của thức thần đó sẽ có hiệu lực.

Recover
Different from Healing. Unaffected by Heal Down effects. Does not trigger effects related to healing, and cannot land Crits.

Hiệu Ứng Kiểm Soát
Freeze, Sleep, Confuse, Taunt, Silence, Bound, Morph, Daze, Provocation, Deep Freeze, Shadowbound, and Frostbound are controlling effects. The last six cannot be dispelled. Frostbound cannot be removed.

Undying Flame Phòng Thủ Đặc Biệt Lửa: 0
Exclusive effect.
The flame may be distinguished, but my heart shall burn for eternity! Removes all buffs and debuffs on her. She stays on the battlefield until the last non-summoned ally entity is KO'd. When she is in this state, she gains 100% Crit RES, 100% increased DEF, and immunity to debuffs, but she's unable to take actions.
Upon entering this state, she immediately recovers 50% of her HP. When her HP reaches her max HP, she's resurrected, resetting the Holy Fire cooldown and changing the skill to Scorching Fire.

Kỹ năng 3: Fire Rite
Sát Thương Lửa: 0

Launches an attack on all enemies, dealing damage equal to 72% of her ATK. Then continues to inflict strikes on all enemies at the cost of her HP equal to 25% of her max HP and 1 orb, dealing damage equal to 43% of her ATK on each strike, until her HP drops to 50% or there're not enough orbs or Wish Power.
Using the skill cannot heal or Recover HP for herself, or trigger HP Steal.

  • Lv.2: Reduces HP cost to 21%
  • Lv.3: Reduces HP cost to 17%
  • Lv.4: Reduces HP cost to 13%
  • Lv.5: If Fire Rite inflicts 5 strikes, her attack ignores DEF by 30 (max 5 stacks)

Recover
Different from Healing. Unaffected by Heal Down effects. Does not trigger effects related to healing, and cannot land Crits.
