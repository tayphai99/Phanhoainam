Jinkougyou

Skill 1: Guiding Fragrance
Damage Orbs: 0

Shapes a wisp of incense smoke into a blade and attacks 1 enemy before it dissipates, dealing damage equal to 100% of his ATK.

  • Lv.2: Increases damage to 105%.
  • Lv.3: Increases damage to 110%.
  • Lv.4: Increases damage to 115%.
  • Lv.5: Increases damage to 125%.

Skill 2: Dream Binder
Support Special Orbs: 3 Effective Exclusively

The echoes of the incense from a distant memory drift out from the divine censer to create a Scented Realm that's Sustainable for 3 turns. While the Scented Realm is active, at the end of each other non-summoned ally entity's turn, Jinkougyou gains 1 Devout Mind stack. Jinkougyou has a 40% Tỷ Lệ Cơ Bản of inflicting Soul Binder when he attacks enemies.

  • Lv.2: Increases the duration of Scented Realm's effect to 4 sustainable turns.
  • Lv.3: Each Soul Binder stack reduces the enemy's DEF by 10%.
  • Lv.4: Reduces orbs required by 1.
  • Lv.5: Upper Hand: Uses Dream Binder.

Devout Mind
[Buff, Mark] When stacked 5 times, consumes current stacks to inflict indirect damage to all enemies equal to 43% of his ATK.

Soul Binder
[Debuff, Mark] Each stack reduces the target's starting DEF by 5%. Max 3 stacks. When stacked 3 times, consumes current stacks, converting them to Distracted and inflicting indirect damage to the enemy equal to 231% of his ATK.

Distracted
[Debuff, Mark] When the Move Bar of the unit with this mark reaches the top of the turn order, removes this mark to skip their turn. The first time the mark is obtained, permanently reduces the target's starting DEF by 35%.

Effective Exclusively
If multiple shikigami of the same name are in your lineup, only one of those shikigami's skills will be effective.

Sustainable
Represents the turns a status or a mark lasts is counted by its source rather than its target.

Tỷ Lệ Cơ Bản
Tỷ lệ bị ảnh hưởng bởi Hiệu Ứng Chính Xác (Effect HIT).

Skill 3: Bodhicitta
Damage Orbs: 3

I will contain the evil within my body to grant you a long and peaceful dream. Attacks all enemies, dealing Indirect Damage equal to 80% of his ATK.

  • Lv.2: Increases damage to 165%.
  • Lv.3: Increases damage to 172%.
  • Lv.4: Increases damage to 180%.
  • Lv.5: Increases damage to 190%.

Distracted
[Debuff, Mark] When the Move Bar of the unit with this mark reaches the top of the turn order, removes this mark to skip their turn. The first time the mark is obtained, permanently reduces the target's starting DEF by 35%.

Indirect Damage
Damage type. Does not trigger soul effects, cannot be shared, and is guaranteed to land critical hits on enemies with 0 DEF.
