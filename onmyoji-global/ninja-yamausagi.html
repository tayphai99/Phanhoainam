Ninja Yamausagi

Kỹ năng 1: Ninja Kick!
Sát Thương Lửa: 0

Gets serious and launches a 2-strike attack on 1 enemy with flying kick, dealing damage equal to 50% of her ATK on each strike.

  • Lv.2: Tăng sát thương lên 53%.
  • Lv.3: Tăng sát thương lên 56%.
  • Lv.4: Tăng sát thương lên 59%.
  • Lv.5: Tăng sát thương lên 65%.

Kỹ năng 2: Ninja Formation!
Sát Thương Hỗ Trợ Lửa: 2 Hiệu Quả Duy Nhất

Active skill: Places 3 Ninja Mines by the enemies' feet. When a Ninja Mine is detonated, if she's not under Controlling Effects or Banished, she uses Ninja Dance!! and gains a Ninja Tricks chapter.
Whenever non-summoned enemy entities take 6 turns, dispels 1 Controlling Effect from her; if there're no Controlling Effects to dispel and she's not Unable to Take Actions, she uses Ninja Formation! that places 2 Ninja Mines once free of orb cost.

  • Lv.2: Damage from Ninja Mines ignores DEF by 150.
  • Lv.3: Increases Ninja Dance!!'s damage boost to 20%.
  • Lv.4: When a Ninja Mine deals damage, reduces the target's Effect RES by 30% for 1 turn.
  • Lv.5: Reduces the enemy turns required to use Ninja Formation! free of orb cost to 5 turns.

Ninja Tricks
[Buff, Mark] Max 9 chapters. Each chapter increases the damage of Ninja Scurrying Steps! by 20%. After using Ninja Scurrying Steps! all chapters are consumed.

Hiệu Quả Duy Nhất
Nếu có nhiều thức thần cùng tên trong đội hình của bạn, chỉ một trong các kỹ năng của thức thần đó sẽ có hiệu lực.

Ninja Mine
Max 9 mines. Placed on the 50% position of the turn order gauge. Detonated when an enemy passes by, dealing damage equal to 150% of her ATK. A mine is removed after it's detonated.

Hiệu Ứng Kiểm Soát
Freeze, Sleep, Confuse, Taunt, Silence, Bound, Morph, Daze, Provocation, Deep Freeze, Shadowbound, and Frostbound are controlling effects. The last six cannot be dispelled. Frostbound cannot be removed.

Banish
Banished target is unable to take actions, they cannot be selected, their Move Bar is locked, their passive skills and Souls equipped won't take effect, they are immune to damage, healing effects, buffs and debuffs.

Unable to Take Actions
Units that are Banished or inflicted with controlling effects, including Freeze, Sleep, Daze, Morph, Deep Freeze, Shadowbound, and Frostbound are unable to take actions.

Ninja Dance!! Hỗ Trợ Lửa: 2
Raises the Move Bar of all allies by 10% and increases their ATK by 10% for 2 turns

Kỹ năng 3: Ninja Scurrying Steps!
Sát Thương Điều Khiển Lửa: 3

Has a 10% Tỷ Lệ Cơ Bản of inflicting Morph lasting 1 turn on the enemy. Then summons mines to attack all enemies, dealing damage equal to 160% of her ATK.

  • Lv.2: Using the skill also increases her Effect HIT by 30% for 2 turns
  • Lv.3: Increases the base chance of inflicting Morph to 20%.
  • Lv.4: Using the skill also places a Ninja Mine
  • Lv.5: Increases the base chance of inflicting Morph to 30%

Tỷ Lệ Cơ Bản
Tỷ lệ bị ảnh hưởng bởi Hiệu Ứng Chính Xác (Effect HIT).

Biến Hình
Không thể bị xua tan. Ngăn chặn các kỹ năng bị động của thức thần có hiệu lực. Mục tiêu biến hình không thể thực hiện hành động.

Ninja Mine
Max 9 mines. Placed on the 50% position of the turn order gauge. Detonated when an enemy passes by, dealing damage equal to 150% of her ATK. A mine is removed after it's detonated.
