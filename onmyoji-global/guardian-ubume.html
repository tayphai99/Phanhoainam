Guardian Ubume

Kỹ năng 1: Ink Wind
Sát Thương Lửa: 0

Moves like a feather and unsheathes her sword to attack 1 enemy, dealing damage equal to 100% of her ATK. After 3 normal attacks, the next normal attack will be replaced with Ink-shadow Slash, except it deals 30% less damage. At the start of battle, she's regarded as having used 2 normal attacks.

  • Lv.2: Tăng sát thương lên 105%.
  • Lv.3: Tăng sát thương lên 110%.
  • Lv.4: Tăng sát thương lên 115%.
  • Lv.5: Tăng sát thương lên 125%. Damage will no longer be reduced by 30% for every 3 normal attacks used.

Kỹ năng 2: Ink Shadow
Hỗ Trợ Bị Động Hiệu Quả Duy Nhất

At the start of her turn, gains 2 Feathery Tutelage stacks.

  • This skill cannot be leveled up.

Feathery Tutelage
[Genera|, Mark]Guardian Ubume is guaranteed to assist an ally the first time they uses a normal attack then consume a Feathery Tutelage stack. After assisting the ally, they gain a Feathery Caring stack.

Feathery Caring
[Buff, MarkJPermanently increases ATK by 10%. Stacks up to 5 times.

Hiệu Quả Duy Nhất
Nếu có nhiều thức thần cùng tên trong đội hình của bạn, chỉ một trong các kỹ năng của thức thần đó sẽ có hiệu lực.

Kỹ năng 3: Ink-shadow Slash
Sát Thương Lửa: 3

Guardian Ubume becomes an Ink-shadow and launches an attack on all enemies, dealing damage equal to 100% of her ATK. She then commands her umbrella to inflict a 3-strike attack on target enemy, dealing damage equal to 62% of her ATK on each strike. If the target enemy is KO'd by the Ink-shadow, her umbrella will switch to attack the enemy with the highest HP instead.

  • Lv.2: Increases lnk-shadow's damage to 110% and her damage to the target enemy to 66%.
  • Lv.3: Increases lnk-shadow's damage to 120% and her damage to the target enemy to 70%.
  • Lv.4: Increases Ink-shadow's damage to 130% and her damage to the target enemy to 74%.
