Waverider Lord Arakawa

Kỹ năng 1: River's Breath
Sát Thương Lửa: 0

Forms water into a torrent to attack 1 enemy, dealing damage equal to 100% of her ATK.

  • Lv.2: Tăng sát thương lên 105%.
  • Lv.3: Tăng sát thương lên 110%.
  • Lv.4: Tăng sát thương lên 115%.
  • Lv.5: Tăng sát thương lên 125%.

Kỹ năng 2: River's Fury
Xua Tan Tăng Tốc Phòng Thủ Lửa: 0

When he's KO'd, his body becomes Unflinching until the end of battle.
(Hiệu Quả Duy Nhất) When an ally takes damage, if the damage is over 30% of their max HP, raises his own Move Bar by 50% and Removes 1 Controlling Effect on himself. Triggers once each turn.
Active skill: Gains a Sea Fury stack. All allies gain the damage reduction effects of Unflinching. Sustainable for 1 turn.

  • Lv.2: Increases sea wave damage to 22% of allied shikigami's starting ATK.
  • Lv.3: Sea Fury effects won't disappear when he's KO'd.
  • Lv.4: When he's KO'd, heals the HP of all allies by 125% of his HP.
  • Lv.5: The damage multiplier of each sea wave strike is equal to 120% of the last one (the damage multiplier can be increased up to 5 times).

Unflinching
[General, Mark] Prevents revival effects from triggering on him. Prevents him from being revived. Prevents his spot from being taken. Reduces damage taken by all allies by 10%. Each 300 starting ATK he has grants an additional 1% damage reduction, up to a total of 30% damage reduction. He continues to exist as a soul upon being KO'd. On his action turn, he harnesses sea waves to attack all enemies, dealing damage equal to 20% of the total starting ATK of allied shikigami. (The total ATK counted this way won't exceed 120% of his starting ATK. This is an exclusive effect and the damage doesn't trigger his Soul effects or the enemies'.)

Sea Fury
[Buff, Mark] Each stack increases damage by 20% and SPD by 30. Stacks up to 3 times.

Hiệu Quả Duy Nhất
Nếu có nhiều thức thần cùng tên trong đội hình của bạn, chỉ một trong các kỹ năng của thức thần đó sẽ có hiệu lực.

Remove
Removes status and effects that cannot be removed.

Hiệu Ứng Kiểm Soát
Freeze, Sleep, Confuse, Taunt, Silence, Bound, Morph, Daze, Provocation, Deep Freeze, Shadowbound, and Frostbound are controlling effects. The last six cannot be dispelled. Frostbound cannot be removed.

Kỹ năng 3: Wavebreaker Slash
Sát Thương Phòng Thủ Lửa: 3

Cleaves through the water and strikes 1 enemy with a towering wave, dealing damage equal to 287% of his ATK. If the enemy is KO'd, he loses a Sea Fury stack.

  • Lv.2: Tăng sát thương lên 315%.
  • Lv.3: Target is regarded as being inflicted with Isolation.
  • Lv.4: Tăng sát thương lên 335%.
  • Lv.5: When he has 3 Sea Fury stacks, his DEF is reduced by 20% and his damage is increased by 40%. After using the skill, all allies gain the damage reduction effects of Unflinching. Sustainable for 1 turn.
