Mishige

Kỹ năng 1: Gourmet Charge
Sát Thương Lửa: 0

Commands Porkie the piglet to crash into 1 enemy, dealing damage equal to 100% of her ATK.

  • Lv.2: Tăng sát thương lên 105%.
  • Lv.3: Tăng sát thương lên 110%.
  • Lv.4: Tăng sát thương lên 115%.
  • Lv.5: Tăng sát thương lên 125%.

Kỹ năng 2: Ready to Feast
Hỗ Trợ Đặc Biệt Lửa: 2 Hiệu Quả Duy Nhất

Gains 1 Grain Reserve stack at the end of a non-summoned ally entity's turn if they're not Unable to Take Actions.
Active skill: Gains 2 Grain Reserve stacks and increases the SPD of other allies by 30, then gains Huge Appetite for 1 turn.

  • This skill cannot be leveled up.

Grain Reserve
[Buff, Mark] Stacks up to 10 times. Each stack increases ATK and Crit DMG by 10% and 5%, respectively. Cannot Assist or receive Call to Arms if there are less than 6 stacks. Won't reset when a round ends.

Huge Appetite
[Buff, Mark] Can also gain 1 Grain Reserve stack at the end of a non-summoned enemy entity's turn.

Hiệu Quả Duy Nhất
Nếu có nhiều thức thần cùng tên trong đội hình của bạn, chỉ một trong các kỹ năng của thức thần đó sẽ có hiệu lực.

Unable to Take Actions
Units that are Banished or inflicted with controlling effects, including Freeze, Sleep, Daze, Morph, Deep Freeze, Shadowbound, and Frostbound are unable to take actions.

Kỹ năng 3: Appetite Stimulated
Sát Thương Lửa: 3

Unlocked when there are 6 Grain Reserve stacks. Launches an attack on the enemy with the highest HP, dealing damage equal to 100% of ATK. Then inflicts 8 strikes on random enemies, dealing damage equal to 33% of ATK on each strike. 4 Grain Reserve stacks are consumed after the attack.

  • Lv.2: Increases first attack damage to 110%.
  • Lv.3: Increases first attack damage to 120%.
  • Lv.4: Increases first attack damage to 130%.
  • Lv.5: Increases first attack damage to 150%.

Hiệu Ứng Kiểm Soát
Freeze, Sleep, Confuse, Taunt, Silence, Bound, Morph, Daze, Provocation, Deep Freeze, Shadowbound, and Frostbound are controlling effects. The last six cannot be dispelled. Frostbound cannot be removed.

Appetite Stimulated: Voracious Sát Thương Lửa: 0
Enhanced Appetite Stimulated. The skill's level is equal to the skill level of Appetite Stimulated. When there are 10 Grain Reserve stacks, immediately uses the skill free of orbs; if under controlling effect, consumes 5 Grain Reserve stacks to first dispel or remove all controlling effects.
Launches an attack on the enemy with the highest HP, dealing damage equal to 180% of ATK, and inflicts True Damage equal to 40% of the damage dealt evenly on other enemies. Then inflicts 9 strikes on random enemies, dealing damage equal to 33% of ATK on each strike. All Grain Reserve stacks are consumed after the attack.
  • Lv.2: Increases first damage to 200%.
  • Lv.3: Increases True Damage inflicted to 60% of first damage.
  • Lv.4: Increases first damage to 230%.
  • Lv.5: Increases True Damage inflicted to 80% of first damage.
