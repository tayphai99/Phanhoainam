Jr Ootengu

Kỹ năng 1: Wind Coil
Sát Thương Lửa: 0

Forms the wind into a vortex of feathers to assault the enemy, dealing damage equal to 100% of his ATK.

  • Lv.2: Tăng sát thương lên 105%.
  • Lv.3: Tăng sát thương lên 110%.
  • Lv.4: Tăng sát thương lên 115%.
  • Lv.5: Tăng sát thương lên 125%.

Kỹ năng 2: Growing Feathers
Sát Thương Hiệu Ứng Có Lợi Bị Động

When he attacks with his skill, increases the skill's damage by 10% for each consecutive hit dealt to the same target.
When inflicted with a Controlling Effect, gains Soars into the clouds. for 2 turns. When dealing damage, gains Chasing the Wind (stacks up to 80 layers). For each 40 layers of Chasing the Wind gained, the orb cost of Feather Blade Wind is reduced by 1 orb.

  • This skill cannot be leveled up.

Soars into the clouds
[Buff, Mark] Increases SPD by 100%.

Chasing the Wind
[General, Mark] SPD increased by 1

Hiệu Ứng Kiểm Soát
Freeze, Sleep, Confuse, Taunt, Silence, Bound, Morph, Daze, Provocation, Deep Freeze, Shadowbound, and Frostbound are controlling effects. The last six cannot be dispelled. Frostbound cannot be removed.

Kỹ năng 3: Feather Blade Wind
Sát Thương Lửa: 3

Commands a hurricane to rain blades onto 1 enemy to inflict 5 continuous attacks, dealing damage equal to 40% of his ATK on each attack. The hurricane then splits into up to 2 small hurricanes based on the number of enemies to inflict random attacks on other enemies, dealing damage equal to 40% of his ATK on each attack.

  • Lv.2: Tăng sát thương lên 42%.
  • Lv.3: Tăng sát thương lên 44%.
  • Lv.4: Tăng sát thương lên 46%.
  • Lv.5: Tăng sát thương lên 50%.
