Yuki

Kỹ năng 1: Snow Chaser
Sát Thương Điều Khiển Lửa: 0

Draws Snow Chaser from its sheath and slashes 1 enemy, dealing damage equal to 80% of his ATK.

  • Lv.2: Tăng sát thương lên 84%.
  • Lv.3: Tăng sát thương lên 88%.
  • Lv.4: Tăng sát thương lên 92%.
  • Lv.5: Tăng sát thương lên 100%.

Kỹ năng 2: Snow Weaver
Điều Khiển Đặc Biệt Bị Động

When attacking or dealing Transferred Damage, has a 45% Tỷ Lệ Cơ Bản of inflicting Freeze lasting 1 - 2 turns on the enemy. Breaks the ice around enemies with Freeze, Deep Freeze, or Frostbound, increasing his damage by 300% and removing Freeze from them.

  • This skill cannot be leveled up.

Transferred Damage
Damage type. Does not land critical hits. Does not trigger the effect of Soul Edge. Transferred by some shikigami skills, including Shouzu's Water Circuit.

Tỷ Lệ Cơ Bản
Tỷ lệ bị ảnh hưởng bởi Hiệu Ứng Chính Xác (Effect HIT).

Freeze
Unable to take actions.

Deep Freeze
Unable to take actions. SPD reduced by 20.

Frostbound
Unable to take actions until Frosty Yuki Onna's next turn. Cannot be reflected back, dispelled, or removed.

Kỹ năng 3: December Snow
Sát Thương Điều Khiển Lửa: 3

Draws Snow Chaser and launches a 3-strike attack at 1 enemy, dealing damage equal to 80% of his ATK on each strike. Increases his Effect HIT by 200% until the end of the turn.

  • Lv.2: Tăng sát thương lên 84%.
  • Lv.3: Tăng sát thương lên 88%.
  • Lv.4: Tăng sát thương lên 92%.
  • Lv.5: Tăng sát thương lên 100%.
