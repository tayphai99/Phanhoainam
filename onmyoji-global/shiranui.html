Shiranui

Kỹ năng 1: Debut
Sát Thương Lửa: 0

Dances to the music and sends off butterflies to launch a 2-strike attack on 1 enemy, dealing damage equal to 50% of her ATK on each strike.
When she's in the Sorrowful state, this skill is changed to Finale, with its skill level equal to the skill level of Debut.

  • Lv.2: Tăng sát thương lên 52%.
  • Lv.3: Tăng sát thương lên 54%.
  • Lv.4: Tăng sát thương lên 56%.
  • Lv.5: Tăng sát thương lên 60%.
Finale Sát Thương Lửa: 0
Stokes the flames and sends over two fire butterflies to launch a 2-strike attack on 1 enemy, dealing damage equal to 62% of her ATK on each strike.
  • Lv.2: Tăng sát thương lên 64%.
  • Lv.3: Tăng sát thương lên 66%.
  • Lv.4: Tăng sát thương lên 68%.
  • Lv.5: Tăng sát thương lên 72%.

Kỹ năng 2: Silhouette of Sorrow
Hỗ Trợ Hiệu Ứng Bất Lợi Lửa: 0 Hiệu Quả Duy Nhất

If an ally uses a normal attack or is unable to take actions on their turn, Shiranui has a 50% chance of performing a dance at the end of that turn, spending 5% of her current HP to increase the Effect RES of the ally by 50% for 1 turn.
Active skill: This can only be used when her HP drops below 50%. Enters the Sorrowful state and uses Blazing Flame without spending any orbs.
When she's in the Sorrowful state, this skill is changed to Song of Sorrow, with its skill level equal to the skill level of Silhouette of Sorrow.

  • Lv.2: Increases Effect RES to 60%.
  • Lv.3: Increases Effect RES to 70%.
  • Lv.4: Increases Effect RES to 80%.
  • Lv.5: While the Sparkling Flame Realm is active, increases her chance of performing a dance to 100%.

Sorrowful
[Buff, Mark] Increases Crit by 30%. At the end of an ally or an enemy's turn, recovers her HP by 40% of the HP lost. Reduces her max HP by 15% at the end of her turn.
Shiranui's attack inflicts Blaze of Sorrow. When she attacks a target inflicted with Blaze of Sorrow, it is consumed to deal double damage. When she's in this state, Recovers her HP by 100%, and removes all buffs and debuffs on her.

Hiệu Quả Duy Nhất
Nếu có nhiều thức thần cùng tên trong đội hình của bạn, chỉ một trong các kỹ năng của thức thần đó sẽ có hiệu lực.

Song of Sorrow Sát Thương Hỗ Trợ Hiệu Ứng Bất Lợi Bị Động
Shiranui increases her SPD by 20.
  • Lv.2: Each orb increases SPD by 5.
  • Lv.3: Increases Effect RES by 20%.
  • Lv.4: Each orb increases Effect RES by 5%
  • Lv.5: After using Blazing Flame actively, for each orb you have left, inflicts damage equal to 46% of her ATK on a random enemy.

Kỹ năng 3: Blazing Sky
Hỗ Trợ Đặc Biệt Lửa: 2 Hiệu Quả Duy Nhất

Forms a Sparkling Flame Realm lasting 2 turns. While the realm is active, all allies' damage dealt and SPD are increased by 10% and 25, respectively, and they gain a 10% damage reduction.
When an ally in the realm uses a normal attack during their own turn, they have a 50% chance of using a normal attack on the same target again. The extra normal attack launched does not trigger the attacker's passive skills or the target's Soul effects and passive skills.
When she's in the Sorrowful state, this skill is changed to Blazing Flame, with its skill level equal to the skill level of Blazing Sky.

  • Lv.2: The extra normal attack launched ignores DEF by 30% and has a 200 HP Steal.
  • Lv.3: While the realm is active, each orb increases damage by 2% and damage reduction by 2%.
  • Lv.4: Increases the chance of using a normal attack an extra time to 100%.
  • Lv.5: Upper Hand: Uses Blazing Sky.

Hiệu Quả Duy Nhất
Nếu có nhiều thức thần cùng tên trong đội hình của bạn, chỉ một trong các kỹ năng của thức thần đó sẽ có hiệu lực.

Upper Hand
Take action at the start of battle.

Blazing Flame Sát Thương Lửa: 4
Performs a dance of flame and attacks all enemies, dealing damage equal to 125% of her ATK.
  • Lv.2: Tăng sát thương lên 132%.
  • Lv.3: Tăng sát thương lên 138%.
  • Lv.4: Tăng sát thương lên 144%.
  • Lv.5: For each ally lost, reduces orbs required by 1.
