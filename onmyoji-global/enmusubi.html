Enmusubi

Kỹ năng 1: Moonlit Flower
Sát Thương Lửa: 0

This is your bond with a goddess' wrath! Wields a moonlit flower to strike... Summons a crushing mark of bond to smash 1 enemy, dealing damage equal to 100% of her ATK.

  • Lv.2: Tăng sát thương lên 105%.
  • Lv.3: Tăng sát thương lên 110%.
  • Lv.4: Tăng sát thương lên 115%.
  • Lv.5: Also raises her Move Bar by 20%.

Kỹ năng 2: Flower Moon
Đặc Biệt Bị Động Hiệu Quả Duy Nhất

At the start of another ally's turn, grants them 1 Knot of a random type (4 in total). If the Knot they received is of the same type as the one granted to the last ally who performed an action (other than Enmusubi), a match is formed. You gain I orb immediately and Enmusubi gains a layer of Fateful Bond. If a match is not formed, Enmusubi gains a layer of I'm Furious.

  • This skill cannot be leveled up.

Fateful Bond
[General, Mark] Stackable for up to 7 layers. When she uses Blessed Bond, each layer of Fateful Bond increases the damage dealt by all allies by an additional 5% and the healing efficiency by 1% of her max HP.

I'm Furious
[General, Mark] When she gains 5 layers, consumes all layers to dispel or remove all controlling effects on her and lowers the Move Bar of all enemies by 25%. She loses 2 layers when a match is formed.

Hiệu Quả Duy Nhất
Nếu có nhiều thức thần cùng tên trong đội hình của bạn, chỉ một trong các kỹ năng của thức thần đó sẽ có hiệu lực.

Kỹ năng 3: Blessed Bond
Hỗ Trợ Chữa Trị Lửa: 2

Blesses 1 ally and grants them Shelter. Also boosts the morale of all non-summoned entity allies, increasing their damage by 40% until the start of her next turn and healing them by 9% of her max HP.

  • Lv.2: When she boosts the morale of her allies, if she has at least 1 layer of Fateful Bond, 50% of excess HP healed will be converted into a shield that lasts 1 turn.
  • Lv.3: When she boosts the morale of her allies, if she has at least 3 layers of Fateful Bond, their SPD will also increase by 30 until the start of her next turn.
  • Lv.4: If she has 7 layers of Fateful Bond, also grants Flame Reignited to the blessed ally when she blesses another ally, and she gains Shelter.
  • Lv.5: Upper Hand: Gains Shelter.

Flame Reignited
[General, Mark] Takes an extra turn after this turn ends. During the extra turn, skills cost 3 orbs less to use.

Shelter
Negates all controlling effects and Banish inflicted by a single skill.

Upper Hand
Take action at the start of battle.
