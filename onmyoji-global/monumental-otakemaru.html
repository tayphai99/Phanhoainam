Monumental Otakemaru

Kỹ năng 1: Mountain's Shadow: Erode
Sát Thương Hiệu Ứng Bất Lợi Lửa: 0

Wields Kenmyoren and unleashes a wave of energy at 1 enemy, dealing damage equal to 100% of his ATK and inflicts Mountain's Erosion on the enemy.

  • Lv.2: Tăng sát thương lên 105%.
  • Lv.3: Tăng sát thương lên 110%.
  • Lv.4: Tăng sát thương lên 115%.
  • Lv.5: Tăng sát thương lên 125%.

Mountain's Erosion
[Debuff, Status] Reduces overall ATK by 15% and healing effects received by 30%. Converts to Mountain's Crush after taking an action.

Mountain's Crush
[Debuff, Mark] inflicts Daze lasting until the next action, during which the overall SPD is reduced by 30%.

Kỹ năng 2: Master of the Mountain
Đặc Biệt Lửa: 0 Hiệu Quả Duy Nhất

Enters the Soul-controlling form for 2 turns, increasing his Effect RES by 100%. Gains a Mountain's Boon stack when another ally uses a Skill.

If in the Soul-controlling form, when 3 or more non-summoned entities are KO'd, all buffs and debuffs on him are removed. He then enters the Reincarnation form, increasing his SPD by 100 and reducing his damage taken by 50%. Also gains immunity to debuffs and Banish. He uses Obliterate Evils automatically on his action turns.

  • Lv.2: Reduces his single-target damage taken by 50% when he's in the Soul-controlling form.
  • Lv.3: When a non-summoned entity is KO'd while he's in the Soul-controlling form, recovers his HP by 30% of his max HP.
  • Lv.4: Soul-controlling form lasts 3 turns.
  • Lv.5: Upper Hand: Uses Master of the Mountain.

Mountain's Boon
[Buff, Mark]Stacks up to 4 times. Each stack reduces Forgotten End's orb cost by 1 orb. After using Forgotten End, all Mountain's Boon stacks are consumed.

Hiệu Quả Duy Nhất
Nếu có nhiều thức thần cùng tên trong đội hình của bạn, chỉ một trong các kỹ năng của thức thần đó sẽ có hiệu lực.

Obliterate Evils Lửa: 0

Kỹ năng 3: Forgotten End
Sát Thương Hiệu Ứng Bất Lợi Lửa: 4

Unleashes a wave of rocks to attack all enemies, dealing damage equal to 180% of his ATK. If he's in the Soul-controlling form, also inflicts Mountain's Erosion on all enemies.

  • Lv.2: Tăng sát thương lên 200%.
  • Lv.3: Tăng sát thương lên 220%.
  • Lv.4: Tăng sát thương lên 240%.
  • Lv.5: Before dealing damage, dispels a buff from each of his enemies.

Mountain's Erosion
[Debuff, Status] Reduces overall ATK by 15% and healing effects received by 30%. Converts to Mountain's Crush after taking an action.

Mountain's Crush
[Debuff, Mark] inflicts Daze lasting until the next action, during which the overall SPD is reduced by 30%.
