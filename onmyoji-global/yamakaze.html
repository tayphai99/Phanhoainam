Yamakaze

Kỹ năng 1: Wind
Sát Thương Lửa: 0

Charges at 1 enemy and launches a 2-strike attack, dealing damage equal to 76% of his ATK with each strike. The strikes are guaranteed to land critical hits if the HP of the target is below 35%.

  • Lv.2: Tăng sát thương lên 80%.
  • Lv.3: Tăng sát thương lên 84%.
  • Lv.4: Tăng sát thương lên 87%.
  • Lv.5: Tăng sát thương lên 95%.

Kỹ năng 2: Violent
Tăng Tốc Hiệu Ứng Có Lợi Bị Động

When a Controlling Effect on him is dispelled or is lifted by ending a turn, if he's not under any Controlling Effects, then raises his Move Bar by 35% and grants him Trapped Beast lasting 1 turn.

  • Lv.2: Increases Trapped Beast's ATK boost effect to 15%.
  • Lv.3: Increases Trapped Beast's ATK boost effect to 20%.
  • Lv.4: Increases Trapped Beast's ATK boost effect to 25%.
  • Lv.5: If any enemy's HP is below 35%, the Move Bar-raising effect is increased to 70%.

Trapped Beast
[Buff, Status] Increases ATK by 10% and Effect RES by 50%.

Hiệu Ứng Kiểm Soát
Freeze, Sleep, Confuse, Taunt, Silence, Bound, Morph, Daze, Provocation, Deep Freeze, Shadowbound, and Frostbound are controlling effects. The last six cannot be dispelled. Frostbound cannot be removed.

Kỹ năng 3: Slash
Sát Thương Hiệu Ứng Bất Lợi Lửa: 3

Channels all of his power to attack 1 enemy before attacking the enemy with the proportionally lowest HP, dealing damage equal to 132% of his ATK on each hit and inflicting Laceration (dealing Indirect Damage equal to 12% of their current HP + 88% of Yamakaze's ATK (max at 320% of Yamakaze's ATK)) lasting 1 turn on the targets. Both attacks are guaranteed to land critical hits.

  • Lv.2: Tăng sát thương lên 139%.
  • Lv.3: Tăng sát thương lên 145%.
  • Lv.4: Tăng sát thương lên 152%.
  • Lv.5: Inflicts a 100% Heal Down lasting 1 turn when Laceration is lifted.

Laceration
[Debuff, Status] Takes damage at the end of the turn. The damage ignores target's DEF by 600.

Indirect Damage
Damage type. Does not trigger soul effects, cannot be shared, and is guaranteed to land critical hits on enemies with 0 DEF.
