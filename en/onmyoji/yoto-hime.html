Yoto Hime

Skill 1: Cursed Blade
Damage Orbs: 0

Uses her katana to attack 1 target, dealing damage equal to 100% of her ATK.

  • Lv.2: Increases damage to 105%.
  • Lv.3: Increases damage to 110%.
  • Lv.4: Increases damage to 1105%.
  • Lv.5: Increases damage to 125%.

Skill 2: Savage Combo
Damage Orbs: 3

Launches a 6-strike attack on 1 enemy, dealing damage equal to 50% of her ATK on each strike. If the enemy is KO'd before she finishes her 6 strikes, she changes her target to the enemy with the lowest HP, inflicting the remaining strikes. If she's in the Glorious Blade state, she gains Dazzling Cleave with its skill level equal to the skill level of Savage Combo.

  • Lv.2: Increases damage to 52%.
  • Lv.3: Increases damage to 54%.
  • Lv.4: Increases damage to 56%.
  • Lv.5: Increases damage to 58% and inflicts 2 extra attacks when changing targets.

Glorious Blade
[Buff, Mark] Attacks ignore 50% of target's DEF. Each time 8 orbs are used by allies, if she's not under Controlling Effects or Banished, she uses Dazzling Cleave on a random enemy free of orb cost, prioritizing the target she last actively attacked with Dazzling Cleave. The damage multiplier of the attack is halved. The skill's orb count resets after it's triggered.

Dazzling Cleave Damage Orbs: 3
Launches a 6-strike attack on 1 enemy, dealing damage equal to 60% of her ATK on each strike.
  • Lv.2: Increases damage to 65%.
  • Lv.3: Increases damage to 70%.
  • Lv.4: Increases damage to 75%.
  • Lv.5: Increases damage to 80%.

Skill 3: Ghostly Blade
Special Passive

Whenever she lands a critical hit, permanently increases her Crit DMG by 5% (max 100% Crit DMG increase). If her Crit DMG reaches 350%, she enters the Glorious Blade state indefinitely at the beginning of her turn.

  • Lv.2: Increases the Crit DMG boost from critical hits to 6% and increases the Crit DMG boost limit to 120%.
  • Lv.3: Increases the Crit DMG boost from critical hits to 7% and increases the Crit DMG boost limit to 140%.
  • Lv.4: Increases the Crit DMG boost from critical hits to 8% and increases the Crit DMG boost limit to 160%.
  • Lv.5: Increases the Crit DMG boost from critical hits to 10% and increases the Crit DMG boost limit to 200%.

Glorious Blade
[Buff, Mark] Attacks ignore 50% of target's DEF. Each time 8 orbs are used by allies, if she's not under Controlling Effects or Banished, she uses Dazzling Cleave on a random enemy free of orb cost, prioritizing the target she last actively attacked with Dazzling Cleave. The damage multiplier of the attack is halved. The skill's orb count resets after it's triggered.

Controlling Effect
Freeze, Sleep, Confuse, Taunt, Silence, Bound, Morph, Daze, Provocation, Deep Freeze, Shadowbound, and Frostbound are controlling effects. The last six cannot be dispelled. Frostbound cannot be removed.
