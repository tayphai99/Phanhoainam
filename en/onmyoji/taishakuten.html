Taishakuten

Skill 1: Celestial Judgment
Damage Orbs: 0

Burns out a sacred lotus to attack 1 enemy, dealing damage equal to 100% of his ATK.

  • Lv.2: Increases damage to 105%.
  • Lv.3: Increases damage to 110%.
  • Lv.4: Increases damage to 115%.
  • Lv.5: Increases damage to 125%.

Skill 2: Flawless Lotus
Damage control Special Orbs: 2 Effective Exclusively

At the start of his turn, if he's not Unable to Take Actions, creates a Ceremony of the King field.

Using the skill has a 100% Base Chance of inflicting Golden Lotus lasting 1 turn on the enemy target.

  • Lv.2: Reduces the starting ATK of the enemy inflicted with Golden Lotus by 80% on their turn.
  • Lv.3: If Ceremony of the King field was not created, raises his Move Bar by 50% at the end of his turn.
  • Lv.4: If the enemy inflicted with Golden Lotus uses a normal attack on their turn, grants 1 orb to you.
  • Lv.5: Increases Golden Lotus duration to 2 turns. If it's inflicted on a monster, it additional inflicts damage equal to 70% of Taishakuten's ATK.

Golden Lotus
[Debuff, Mark] Allows Taishakuten to gain control of a turn. Golden Lotus negates all controlling effects inflicted by a single skill at the cost of 1 turn of its duration. When Taishakuten takes lethal damage, Golden Lotus is removed, blocking the damage and recovering his HP to 5% of his max HP. If it's inflicted on a monster, when the monster takes damage, it additionally inflicts damage equal to 40% of the damage dealt as true damage. The true damage inflicted won't trigger Taishakuten's Soul—set effects. The effect lasts until the battle ends. Taishakuten can only inflict Golden Lotus on up to 1 enemy and himself is immune to this effect.

Effective Exclusively
If multiple shikigami of the same name are in your lineup, only one of those shikigami's skills will be effective.

Unable to Take Actions
Units that are Banished or inflicted with controlling effects, including Freeze, Sleep, Daze, Morph, Deep Freeze, Shadowbound, and Frostbound are unable to take actions.

Base Chance
Chance is affected by Effect HIT.

Ceremony of the King Special Orbs: 0
I shall deliver judgment upon this world!
All allies reduce SPD by 3% for each enemy shikigami and onmyoji in the field.
Raises the Move Bar of the ally at the top of the turn order by 30% at the start of an enemy's turn.
The field ends when Taishakuten is KO'd.

Skill 3: Instant Lotus Bloom
Damage Orbs: 3

A myriad of Lotuses instantly bloom!
Attacks all enemies, dealing damage equal to 138% of his ATK and recovering the HP of himself and the ally with the lowest proportion of HP each by 100% of his ATK.

  • Lv.2: Increases damage to 145%.
  • Lv.3: Increases damage to 152%.
  • Lv.4: Increases damage to 159%.
  • Lv.5: When the inflicted Golden Lotus disappears, removes all control effects on him and uses an Instant Lotus Bloom for no orb cost. The damage doesn't trigger the Soul-set effects and passive skills of all enemies, and instead deals 40% less damage and recovers 40% less HP.
