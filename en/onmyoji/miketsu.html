Miketsu

Skill 1: Divine Arrow
Damage Control Orbs: 0

Attacks 1 enemy, dealing damage equal to 80% of her ATK.
After an enemy takes an action, has a 5% chance of releasing an Arrow of Sealing at the enemy, dealing damage equal to Divine Arrow's damage with a 100% Base Chance of inflicting Silence, Seal, Suppress or 75% Heal Down upon the enemy. Arrow of Sealing does not trigger soul effects or passive skills of the target.

  • Lv.2: Increases damage to 84%.
  • Lv.3: Increases damage to 88%.
  • Lv.4: Increases damage to 92%.
  • Lv.5: Increases damage to 100%.

Base Chance
Chance is affected by Effect HIT.

Silence
Prevents using skills.

Seal
Prevents the effects of shikigami's souls.

Suppress
Prevents shikigami passive skills from taking effect.

Skill 2: Fox Realm
Damage Control Debuff Orbs: 3

Forms a Fox Realm lasting 1 turn. The realm increases her chance of releasing Arrow of Seal to 40% and grants her 4 layers of Amulet stacking up to 12 layers.

  • This skill cannot be leveled up.

Amulet
[General, Mark] Each layer increases the DEF of all allies by 3%, their damage by 2% and their SPD by 1.

Skill 3: Ignite: Magic Arrow
Damage Orbs: 3

Ignites all amulets to attack 1 enemy, dealing damage equal to 195% of her ATK. Consumes all layers of Amulet she has, increasing the damage by 20% for each layer consumed.

  • Lv.2: Increases each Iayer's damage to 21%.
  • Lv.3: Increases each Iayer's damage to 22%.
  • Lv.4: Increases each Iayer's damage to 23%.
  • Lv.5: Increases each Iayer's damage to 25%.

Amulet
[General, Mark] Each layer increases the DEF of all allies by 3%, their damage by 2% and their SPD by 1.
