Crimson Yoto

Skill 1: Flaming Blade
Damage Orbs: 0

Uses her long katana to attack one target, dealing damage equal to 100% of her ATK.

  • Lv.2: Increases damage to 105%.
  • Lv.3: Increases damage to 110%.
  • Lv.4: Increases damage to 115%.
  • Lv.5: Increases damage to 125%.

Skill 2: Relentless
Damage Passive

When her turn ends, Lockeds onto the non-summoned enemy entity currently with the lowest proportion of HP.
Increases her damage by 1% and SPD by 1 for each 1% HP loss on the non-summoned enemy entity with the lowest proportion of HP.

  • Lv.2: Increases damage dealt to the Locked target to 105%.
  • Lv.3: Increases damage dealt to the Locked target to 110%.
  • Lv.4: Increases damage dealt to the Locked target to 115%.
  • Lv.5: When dealing damage to a Locked target, has a 100% Base Chanceof inflicting Isolation.

Locked
[General, Mark] At the beginning of her next turn, consumes the mark and deals damage equal to 100% of her ATK to the locked target. The damage does not trigger soul effects or passive skills and cannot be shared.

Skill 3: Crimson Slash
Damage Orbs: 3

Draws her blade from the rift where the space is torn open and channels all of her power to slash one enemy, dealing damage equal to 295% of her ATK.

  • Lv.2: When the attack KO's a non-summoned enemy entity, she gains Crimson Breath (buff, status), increasing her damage by 15% to a max of 90% increased.
  • Lv.3: When the attack KO's a non-summoned enemy entity, she gains a new turn that does not trigger Locked.
  • Lv.4: When the attack KO's an enemy, she gains a new turn. Using Crimson Slash in the new turn costs no orbs.
  • Lv.5: In the new turn granted, Crimson Slash deals an additional 30% damage. This damage cannot be shared and doesn't trigger the Soul effects or passive skills of the target.
