Takiyasha hime

Skill 1: Moon Slash
Damage Orbs: 0

Wields Moonlight Glaive to attack 1 enemy, dealing damage equal to 100% of her ATK.

  • Lv.2: Increases damage to 105%.
  • Lv.3: Increases damage to 110%.
  • Lv.4: Increases damage to 115%.
  • Lv.5: Increases damage to 125%.

Skill 2: Moon's Secrets
Special CD: 2 Orbs: 0

Toggles through different modes of Moon's Secrets. Adds the effect of Ignis to it, allowing her attacks to ignore DEF by 180 against targets with HP over 50%.
As the skill is leveled up, the effects of Aqua, Aer, and Terra can be unlocked.

  • Lv.2: Unlocks the Aqua effect. Allows each attack of Moon's Secrets to dispel 1 buff from the target. If no buff was dispelled, lowers the target's Move Bar by 5% instead.
  • Lv.3: Unlocks the Aer effect. Allows damage dealt by Moon's Secrets to increase by 30% against targets with HP below 50%.
  • Lv.4: Unlocks the Terra effect. Prevents damage dealt by Moon's Secrets from triggering the target's Soul effects. She gains Moon Shelter for 1 turn, negating controlling effects once.
  • Lv.5: Reduces duration by 1 turn.

Skill 3: Shadowless Sun
Damage Orbs: 3

One of the Moon's Secrets. Commands a legion of ghost knights to charge at all enemies 3 times, dealing damage equal to 12%, 24%, and 99% of her ATK, respectively.
Sleepless Moon is another Moon's Secret.

  • Lv.2: Increases the damage of the third attack to 109%.
  • Lv.3: Increases the damage of the third attack to 118%.
  • Lv.4: Increases the damage of the third attack to 127%.
  • Lv.5: Increases the damage of the third attack to 136%.

  • Lv.3: Increases damage to 55%.
  • Lv.4: Increases damage to 58%.
