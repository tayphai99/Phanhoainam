Shuten Doji

Skill 1: Calabash Spray
Damage Orbs: 0

Sprays 1 enemy with noxious vapors from his calabash, dealing damage equal to 100% of his ATK. Inflicts 1 follow-up attack for each layer of Madness he has. Does not trigger when launching a counter-attack.

  • Lv.2: Increases damage to 105%.
  • Lv.3: Increases damage to 110%.
  • Lv.4: Increases damage to 115%.
  • Lv.5: Increases damage to 125%.

Madness
[Buff, Status] Increases Calabash Spray's attack attempts.

Skill 2: Madness
Special Passive

At Ewe end of his action, has a 50% chance of gaining 1 layer of Madness. When he is attacked, has a 25% chance of gaining 1 layer of Madness.
Madness stacks up to 4 layers.

  • This skill cannot be leveled up.

Madness
[Buff, Status] Increases Calabash Spray's attack attempts.

Skill 3: Healing Rage
Heal Accelerator Special Orbs: 3

Emits a burst of energy, restoring his HP by 30% of his max HP and raising his Move Bar by 50%. He then goes Demoniac for a number of turns (equal to the layers of Madness he has). All layers of Madness are consumed when his Madness state ends.

  • Lv.2: Increases Demoniac's HP Steal effect to 15%.
  • Lv.3: Increases Demoniac's HP Steal effect to 20%.
  • Lv.4: Increases Demoniac's HP Steal effect to 25%.
  • Lv.5: Increases Demoniac's HP Steal effect to 30%.

Demoniac
[Buff, Mark] Grants immunity to all controlling effects and Banish. Attack inflicts 10% HP Steal.

Madness
[Buff, Status] Increases Calabash Spray's attack attempts.
