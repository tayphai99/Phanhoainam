Frosty Yuki Onna

Skill 1: Jade Echoing
Damage Orbs: 0

Creates an ice dart in her hand and throws it at 1 enemy, dealing damage equal to 100% of her ATK.

  • Lv.2: Increases damage to 105%.
  • Lv.3: Increases damage to 110%.
  • Lv.4: Increases damage to 115%.
  • Lv.5: Increases damage to 125%.

Skill 2: Frost Breath
Damage Support Special Orbs: 0 Effective Exclusively

Increases the DEF of all allies by 30%. At the end of a turn (either an ally's or an enemy's; not counting summoned entities), Frosty Yuki Onna creates a Flower of Ice. When an ally shikigami other than herself takes lethal damage, they become Bound in Ice to occupy their original position, but are regarded as KO'd.
Active skill: If there's an ally shikigami Bound in Ice on the battlefield, revives all shikigami Bound in Ice and Recovers their HP to full. After using the skill, it is changed to Spring's Arrival.

  • Lv.2: Increases the damage of Ice Nova against Frostbound enemies by 50%.
  • Lv.3: Each ally Bound in Ice increases her SPD by 50.
  • Lv.4: Ice Nova deals 30% less damage to allies.
  • Lv.5: Gains additional 3 Flowers of Ice at the end of her own turn.

Flower of Ice
[General, Mark] lf she is not unable to take actions, she emits an Ice Nova each time she has 6 stacks, dealing indirect damage to all allies and enemies other than herself equal to 100% of her ATK. This doesn't trigger their passive skills.

Effective Exclusively
If multiple shikigami of the same name are in your lineup, only one of those shikigami's skills will be effective.

Recover
Different from Healing. Unaffected by Heal Down effects. Does not trigger effects related to healing, and cannot land Crits.

Indirect Damage
Damage type. Does not trigger soul effects, cannot be shared, and is guaranteed to land critical hits on enemies with 0 DEF.

Spring's Arrival Support passive
Effective Exclusively. Where the spring breeze blows, everything springs to life. Increases the damage and SPD of all allies by 20% and 20, respectively.

Skill 3: Eternal Winter
Damage Shield Control Orbs: 3

Turns into a cold ice lancer to attack 1 enemy, dealing damage equal to 100% of her ATK and causes them to be Frostbound. She gains Thin Ice. Frostbound ends when she loses Thin Ice.

Thin Ice
[Buff, Mark] A shield that cannot be dispelled. Fails at the start of Frosty Yuki Onna's turn. Absorbs damage equal to 120% of her ATK.

Frostbound
Unable to take actions until Frosty Yuki Onna's next turn. Cannot be reflected back, dispelled, or removed.

Upper Hand
Take action at the start of battle.
