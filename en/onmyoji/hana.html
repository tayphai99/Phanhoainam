Hana

Skill 1: Loyal Flock
Damage Heal Orbs: 0

Commands the birds around her to peck 1 enemy a number of times based on the number of birds she has. Each bird deals damage equal to 40% of her ATK with a 50% chance of restoring the ally with the lowest proportion of HP by 10% of her max HP.
At the start of her turn or when she has no birds, replenish 1 bird to a maximum of 2 birds.
Launching a counter-attack consumes 1 bird.

  • Lv.2: Increases chance of healing to 60%.
  • Lv.3: Increases chance of healing to 70%.
  • Lv.4: Increases chance of healing to 80%.
  • Lv.5: Increases max bird limit to 3.

Skill 2: Fragrant F lowers
Heal Orbs: 3

Restores HP of all allies by 8% of her max HP and grants them Nectar lasting 2 turns, restoring their HP by 7% and 6% in these turns, respectively.

  • Lv.2: icreases healing to 10%.
  • Lv.3: Increases HP restored by Nectar in the next 2 turns to 9% and 8%, respectively.
  • Lv.4: Increases healing to 12%.
  • Lv.5: Increases HP restored by Nectar in the next 2 turns to 1 1% and 10%, respectively.

Skill 3: Painting Realm
Accelerator Support Passive

At the end of her action, has a 20% chance of raising her Move Bar by 20%.
Effective Exclusively: When she or an ally is under a controlling effect, has a 30% chance of consuming 1 bird to negate the Controlling Effect.

  • Lv.2: Increases chance of triggering to 25% when they end their action.
  • Lv.3: Increases Move Bar-raising effect to 25%.
  • Lv.4: Increases chance of triggering to 30% when they end their action.
  • Lv.5: Increases Move Bar-raising effect to 30%.

Effective Exclusively
If multiple shikigami of the same name are in your lineup, only one of those shikigami's skills will be effective.

Controlling Effect
Freeze, Sleep, Confuse, Taunt, Silence, Bound, Morph, Daze, Provocation, Deep Freeze, Shadowbound, and Frostbound are controlling effects. The last six cannot be dispelled. Frostbound cannot be removed.
