Shishio

Skill 1: Forest Vines
Damage Debuff Orbs: 0

Summons vines to attack 1 enemy, dealing damage equal to 100% of his ATK with a 50% Base Chance of inflicting Forest Vines. lasting 2 turns on them.

  • Lv.2: Increases damage to 105% and Base Chance to 60%.
  • Lv.3: Increases damage to 110% and Base Chance to 70%.
  • Lv.4: Increases damage to 115% and Base Chance to 80%.
  • Lv.5: Increases damage to 120% and Base Chance to 100%.

Forest Vines
[Debuff, Status] Reduces Effect RES by 20%.

Base Chance
Chance is affected by Effect HIT.

Skill 2: Gore
Damage Control Decelerator Orbs: 3

Channels his energy into his antlers and charges into 1 enemy, dealing damage equal to 158% of his ATK.
When he has Circle of Life, also lowers their Move Bar by 40%. Has a 100% Base Chance of inflicting Daze lasting 1 turn on them if their Move Bar is already at the bottom.
Inflicting Daze costs a layer of Circle of Life.

  • Lv.2: Increases damage to 166%.
  • Lv.3: Increases damage to 174%. If he has 2 layers of Circle of Life he can attack an extra random enemy. Damage is reduced by 40% for each attack on the same target.
  • Lv.4: creases damage to 182%.
  • Lv.5: Increases damage to 191%. If he has 2 or more layers of Circle of Life, he can attack an extra random enemy for each additional layer. Damage is reduced by 40% for each attack on the same target.

Circle of Life
[Buff, Status] An aura rich with the essence of the forest. Grants Shishio great power.

Base Chance
Chance is affected by Effect HIT.

Daze
Cannot be dispelled. Unable to take actions.

Skill 3: Circle of Life
Special Accelerator Passive

At the end of his turn, gains a layer of Circle of Life (stacks up to 3 layers) if the status is not consumed.
When inflicted with a Controlling Effect, has a 10% chance of reflecting the effect back to a random enemy.
His Move Bar is raised by 20% when an enemy uses an orb skill.

  • Lv.2: Increases chance of reflecting effects by 12%.
  • Lv.3: Increases chance of reflecting effects by 14%.
  • Lv.4: Increases chance of reflecting effects by 16%.
  • Lv.5: Increases chance of reflecting effects by 20%.

Circle of Life
[Buff, Status] An aura rich with the essence of the forest. Grants Shishio great power.

Controlling Effect
Freeze, Sleep, Confuse, Taunt, Silence, Bound, Morph, Daze, Provocation, Deep Freeze, Shadowbound, and Frostbound are controlling effects. The last six cannot be dispelled. Frostbound cannot be removed.
