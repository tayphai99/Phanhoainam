Kaguya

Skill 1: Horai Branch
Damage Orb Orbs: 0

Wields a jeweled branch from Horai and summons spirit energy to strike 1 enemy, dealing damage equal to 100% of her ATK with a 10% chance of removing 1 orb from them.

  • Lv.2: Increases damage to 105%.
  • Lv.3: Increases damage to 110%.
  • Lv.4: Increases damage to 115%.
  • Lv.5: Increases damage to 125%.

Skill 2: Fire Rat
Orb Passive Effective Exclusively

When attacked, has a 30% chance of gaining 1 orb.
If Dragon Jewel protective field is active, the effect also triggers when an ally is attacked. When receiving AoE attacks, multiple strikes do not trigger the effect multiple times.

  • Lv.2: The chance of triggering increases to 35%.
  • Lv.3: The chance of triggering increases to 40%.

Effective Exclusively
If multiple shikigami of the same name are in your lineup, only one of those shikigami's skills will be effective.

Skill 3: Dragon Jewel
Orb Support Orbs: 2

Forms a protective field lasting 2 turns. The field increases all allies' DEF by 15% and their Effect RES by 10% with a 67% chance of granting 1 orb at the start of their turn.
Under her field, if you don't have enough orbs to use a skill, you can still spend all the remaining orbs to use the skill. Each time the effect is triggered, Kaguya sacrifices 5% of her current HP for each orb spent and her field's duration is reduced by 1 turn
Under her field, her Dragon Jewel costs 1 orb less.

  • Lv.2: Increases DEF boost effect to 20%.
  • Lv.3: Increases Effect RES boost effect to 15%.
  • Lv.4: Increases DEF boost effect to 25%.
  • Lv.5: Increases Effect RES boost effect to 20%.
