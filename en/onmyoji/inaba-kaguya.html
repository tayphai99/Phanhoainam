Inaba Kaguya

Skill 1: Crystal Light
Damage Orbs: 0

Forms moonlight into a crystal and attacks 1 enemy, dealing damage equal to 100% of her ATK.

  • Lv.2: increases damage to 105%.
  • Lv.3: increases damage to 110%.
  • Lv.4: increases damage to 115%.
  • Lv.5: increases damage to 125%.

Skill 2: Wishful Night
Support Orbs: 2 Effective Exclusively

Creates a field that lasts 1 turn and gains 3 points of Wish Power. In the field, she gains 80% Effect RES and other allies gain Wish's Protection that's sustainble for 1 turn whenever they use a skill.

  • Lv.2: Orbs generated from Souls are first converted into an equal amount of Wish Power in the field.
  • Lv.3: Gains 3 Wish Power at the start of her turn in the field.
  • Lv.4: increases field effect to 2 turns.
  • Lv.5: Upper Hand: Uses Wishful Night.

Wish Power
[General, Mark] Max storage: 8 points. Replace insufficient orbs with corresponding Wish Power when an ally uses a skill.

Wish's Protection
[Buff, Mark] Grants Crit DMG and DEF equal to 30% of Inaba Kaguya's stats. Max 120% Crit DMG boost and 200 DEF boost.

Effective Exclusively
If multiple shikigami of the same name are in your lineup, only one of those shikigami's skills will be effective.

Upper Hand
Take action at the start of battle.

Skill 3: Reflected Devastation
Damage Orbs: 3

Turns the glowing moon cocoon into a crystal kaleidoscope and attacks all enemies. dealing damage equal to 138% of her ATK and issuing a Call to Arms to allies under Wish's Protection Also reduces the orb cost of Wishful Night the next time she uses it by 1.

  • Lv.2: Each 1 point of Wish Power provided increases her own Crit DMG by 5%. Max 120% Crit DMG boost.
  • Lv.3: Each time she provided Wish Power, creates a shield that absorbs damage equal to 12% of her max HP for each point of Wish Power provided. Max 8 stack. Lasts 2 turns.
  • Lv.4: Ignores enemy Souls and Passive skills if 12 points of Wish Power have been provided.
  • Lv.5: Summons Inaba Rabbit to use Cleansing Moon if 24 points of Wish Power have been provided.

Wish's Protection
[Buff, Mark] Grants Crit DMG and DEF equal to 30% of Inaba Kaguya's stats. Max 120% Crit DMG boost and 200 DEF boost.

Call to Arms
An ally who receives Call to Arms uses a normal attack to attack the same enemy.

Cleansing Moon Damage Orbs: 0
Inaba Rabbit becomes Winged Tsukinohime who launches another attack on all enemies, dealing damage equal to 153% of Inaba Kaguya's ATK. The damage dealt ignores enemy Souls and passive skills.
