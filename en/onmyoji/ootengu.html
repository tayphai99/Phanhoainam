Ootengu

Skill 1: Vortex Assault
Damage Orbs: 0

Shapes the wind into a vortex to assault the enemy, dealing damage equal to 100% of his ATK.

  • Lv.2: Increases damage to 105%.
  • Lv.3: Increases damage to 110%.
  • Lv.4: Increases damage to 115%.
  • Lv.5: Increases damage to 125%.

Skill 2: Wings of Steel
Buff Accelerator Passive

At the start of battle and after his turn ends, gains Shelter.
At the start of his turn, loses Shelter.
When dealing damage, has a 50% chance of gaining Heroic Posture, stackable for up to 10 layers and lasting until the round ends.

  • Lv.2: Heroic Posture stacks up to 30 layers.
  • Lv.3: Heroic Posture stacks up to 50 layers.
  • Lv.4: Heroic Posture stacks up to 80 layers.
  • Lv.5: When Shelter negates a controlling effect, his Move Bar is raised by 50%.

Heroic Posture
[General, Mark] Each layer increases damage by 1%.

Shelter
Negates all controlling effects and Banish inflicted by a single skill.

Skill 3: Blade Storm
Damage Orbs: 3

Commands a storm to rain blades onto all enemies to inflict 4 continuous strikes, dealing damage equal to 37% of his ATK on each strike. At the start of his turn, if an enemy is not inflicted with Feather Blade, inflicts Feather Blade lasting 2 turns on them on the last strike.

  • Lv.2: Increases the damage to 39% and Feather Blade's damage to 19%.
  • Lv.3: Increases the damage to 41% and Feather Blade's damage to 20%.
  • Lv.4: Increases the damage to 43% and Feather Blade's damage to 21%.
  • Lv.5: Increases the damage to 45% and Feather Blade's damage to 22%.

Feather Blade
[General, Mark] When the inflicted target is attacked by Blade Storm, also inflicts damage equal to 18% of his ATK, granting 1 Heroic Posture stack to himself and removing the Feather Blade.
