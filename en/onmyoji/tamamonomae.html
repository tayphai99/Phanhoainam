Tamamo no mae

Skill 1: Soul Burst
Damage Control Orbs: 0

Uses spirit energy to attack 1 enemy, dealing damage equal to 100% of his ATK with a 10% Base Chance of inflicting Confuse lasting 1 turn(s) on them.

  • Lv.2: Increases damage to 105%.
  • Lv.3: Increases damage to 110%.
  • Lv.4: Increases damage to 115%.
  • Lv.5: Increases damage to 125%.

Base Chance
Chance is affected by Effect HIT.

Confuse
Confused enemies are forced to use normal attack to attack a random target on the battlefield when they take their action.

Skill 2: Fox Fire
Damage Orbs: 3

Focuses Fox Fire to attack 1 enemy, dealing damage equal to 263% of his ATK. His damage is reduced by 15% on targets with more than 80% HP.

  • Lv.2: Increases damage to 276%.
  • Lv.3: Damage is increased by 15% on targets with less than 50% HP.
  • Lv.4: Increases damage to 289%.
  • Lv.5: If the enemy is KO'd, uses The Falling without spending any orbs.

Skill 3: The Falling
Damage Orbs: 3

Channels all of his power, dealing damage to all enemies equal to 131% of his ATK. His damage is reduced by 15% on targets with less than 50% HP.

  • Lv.2: Increases damage to 138%.
  • Lv.3: Damage is increased by 15% on targets with more than 80% HP.
  • Lv.4: Increases damage to 145%.
  • Lv.5: If an enemy is KO'd, uses Fox Fire on the enemy with the lowest proportion of HP without spending any orbs.
