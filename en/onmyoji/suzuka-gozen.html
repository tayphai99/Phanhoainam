Suzuka Gozen

Skill 1: Shadowchaser
Damage Orbs: 0

Uses her white hot Shadowchaser to attack 1 enemy, dealing damage equal to 100% of her ATK.

  • Lv.2: Increases damage to 105%.
  • Lv.3: Increases damage to 110%.
  • Lv.4: Increases damage to 115%.
  • Lv.5: Increases damage to 125%.

Skill 2: Mountain's Soul: Ultra
Damage Support Special Orbs: 2

Wields her Nether Bow to attack 1 enemy, dealing damage equal to 211% of her ATK and gaining Obligated Duty until her next turn.

  • Lv.2: After using the skill, the Move Bar of all allies is raised by 10%.
  • Lv.3: As long as she has Obligated Duty, her Effect RES is increased by 50%.
  • Lv.4: As long as she has Obligated Duty, her Crit RES is increased by 100%.
  • Lv.5: When she gains Obligated Duty, grants a new turn to the attacked enemy. If they are not Unable to Take Actions, they are forced to launch a normal attack at Suzuka Gozen. (Summoned entities and monsters won't be affected.)

Obligated Duty
[Buff, Mark] Exclusive effect. Creates a shield, absorbing damage equal to 1 15% of her ATK. While the shield is active, your enemy cannot target your other allies with their skills. The shield can sustain up to 5 attacks. When the shield fails, it is removed along with this status.

Unable to Take Actions
Units that are Banished or inflicted with controlling effects, including Freeze, Sleep, Daze, Morph, Deep Freeze, Shadowbound, and Frostbound are unable to take actions.

Skill 3: Crashing Barrage
Damage Orbs: 4

Combines the power of her two weapons and shoots Shadowchaser with her Nether Bow to launch a 5-strike attack on all enemies, dealing damage equal to 44% of her ATK with each strike. Her attack also inflicts a 10% HP Steal.

  • Lv.2: Increases damage to 47%.
  • Lv.3: Increases damage to 50%.
  • Lv.4: Increases damage to 53%.
  • Lv.5: Each Orb spent by any ally increases her ATK and DEF by 2%. Stacks up to 50 times.
