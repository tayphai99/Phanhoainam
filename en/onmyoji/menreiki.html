Skill 2: Masks of Recurrence
Accelerator Support Buff Passive

Each layer of Mask of Evil on the battlefield increases her SPD by 10 and her damage by 10%. Each layer of Mask of Goodwill increases her Effect RES by 10% and her damage reduction by 10%.
After an ally takes an action, the Mask of Goodwill raises the Move Bar of the ally by 5% before it transforms into a Mask of Evil and transfers to a random enemy (prioritizing enemies without Mask of Evil followed by the enemy with the lowest proportion of HP).

  • Lv.2: Increases Mask of Evil's SPD boost to 15.
  • Lv.3: Increases Mask of Goodwill's Effect RES boost to 20%.
  • Lv.4: Increases Mask of Evil's SPD boost to 20.
  • Lv.5: Increases Move Bar raised by Mask of Goodwill to 10%.
