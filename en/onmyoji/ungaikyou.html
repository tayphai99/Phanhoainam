Ungaikyou

Skill 1: Falling Shadow
Damage Orbs: 0

Casts the power of chaos at 1 enemy, dealing damage equal to 100% of his ATK.
When he's in Yang state, this is changed to Dharma, with its skill level equal to the skill level of Falling Shadow.

  • Lv.2: Increases damage to 105%.
  • Lv.3: Increases damage to 110%.
  • Lv.4: Increases damage to 115%.
  • Lv.5: Increases damage to 125%.
Dharma Damage Accelerator Orbs: 0
Casts the power of an artifact to attack 1 enemy, dealing damage equal to 200% of his DEF. Also raises the Move Bar of another ally at the beginning of the turn order by 20%.
  • Lv.2: Increases damage to 240%.
  • Lv.3: Increases damage to 280%.
  • Lv.4: Increases damage to 320%.
  • Lv.5: Increases damage to 360%.

Skill 2: Shifting of the Sky
Shield Special Orbs: 0

Ungaikyo lives in the Mirror Realm where his two faces are independent on each other. He can change between his Yin/Yang states. His starting HP is halved. His HP in Yin and Yang state are compliment to each other.
Using the skill enters the Yang state and inverts his HP to the Yang state's HP. Also increases his SPD by 100 for 1 turn. Orb cost +2 indefinitely after using the skill each time.
When he's in Yang state, the skill is changed to Shifting of the Stars.

  • This skill cannot be leveled up.

Mirror Realm
Ungaikyo cannot have his HP recovered through healing effects, but 50% of healing effects received will be converted into a shield that lasts 2 turns.

Yin/Yang
Yin/Yang are the two states that belong exclusively to Ungaikyo: When he's in Yin state, his ATK is increased by 200% of his DEF. When he's in Yang state, his DEF is increased by 10% of his ATK. His HP in Yin and Yang state compliment each other. If he takes damage when he's in Yin state, his HP in Yin state will drop and his HP in Yang state will increase, and vice versa. His starting HP is halved.
At the start of battle, if Ungaikyo's starting ATK is 500% times higher than his DEF, he enters Yin state. Otherwise, he enters Yang state.

Shifting of the Stars Special Orbs: 0
Using the skill enters Yin state and inverts his HP. Also increases his SPD by 100 for 1 turn. Orb cost +2 indefinitely after using the skill each time.

Skill 3: World Purge
Damage Debuff Orbs: 3

To cleanse all filth from the world, he attacks all enemies, dealing damage equal to 131% of his ATK. Has a 100% Base Chance of inflicting Mirror's Wrath on all enemies, lasting until the round ends. He then sends a Black Lotus plunging upon 1 enemy, dealing damage equal to 131% of his ATK.
When he's in Yang state, this skill is changed to Painful Life with a skill level equal to his World Purge skill level.

  • Lv.2: Increases the damage of both Cleansing and Black Lotus to 135%.
  • Lv.3: Increases the damage of both Cleansing and Black Lotus to 139%.
  • Lv.4: Increases the damage of both Cleansing and Black Lotus to 145%.
  • Lv.5: For each 30% HP reduced when he's in Yin state, inflicts an additional stage of Black Lotus.

Mirror's Wrath
[Debuff, Status] Mirror's Wrath is consumed the next time the target receives a dispel-able buff to negate the buff.

Mirror's Protection
[Buff, Status] When inflicted with debuffs or controlling effects, dispels 1 debuff or controlling effect immediately and raises Move Bar by 10%. The dispel effect can be triggered up to once each turn. When the effect is triggered, recovers the HP of the ally with the lowest proportion of HP by 240% of Ungaikyo's DEF. Target gains Mirror Shield when they lose Mirror's Protection.

Mirror Shield
[Buff, Mark] Shield that absorbs damage equal to 680% of Ungaikyo's DEF. Lasts 2 turns.

Base Chance
Chance is affected by Effect HIT.

Painful Life Dispel Accelerator Orbs: 3
Ungaikyo gains Mirror's Protection when in Yang state.
Using this skill grants Mirror's Protection to 1 ally. Up to 2 allies can have Mirror's Protection at the same time until the round ends (the number of allies with Mirror's Protection won't increase as you deploy more Ungaikyo). Raises the Move Bar of all allies by 20%. All Mirror's Protections are removed after entering Yin state.
  • Lv.2: Increases HP recovery efficiency of vlirror's Protection to 480% of DEF.
  • Lv.3: Increases the amount of debuffs/controlling effects dispelled by Mirror's Protection to 2.
  • Lv.4: Increases the Move Bar-raising effect of Mirror's Protection to 20%.
  • Lv.5: For every 30% of HP reduced in Yang state, the cost of this skill will reduce by 1 orb.
