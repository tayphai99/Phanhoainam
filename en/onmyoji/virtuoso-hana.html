Virtuoso Hana

Skill 1: Plum Blossom Strike
Damage Orbs: 0

Paints a colorful flower branch to attack 1 enemy, dealing damage equal to 100% of her ATK.

  • Lv.2: Increases damage to 105%.
  • Lv.3: Increases damage to 110%.
  • Lv.4: Increases damage to 115%.
  • Lv.5: Increases damage to 125% and grants herself Shelter for 1 turn.

Skill 2: Bound by Ink
Special Passive Effective Exclusively

Grants Inkbound to all allies.

  • Lv.2: Increases Inkbound's healing amount boost to 30%.
  • Lv.3: Inkbound also increases Effect RES by 20%.
  • Lv.4: At the start of battle, Inkbound permanently increases the max HP of all non-summoned ally entities by 10%.
  • Lv.5: When Lifelike Image disappears outside her turn, raises her Move Bar by 30%.

Inkbound
[Buff, Mark]When healed, increases the healing amount by 20%.

Effective Exclusively
If multiple shikigami of the same name are in your lineup, only one of those shikigami's skills will be effective.

Skill 3: Picturesque Life
Special Orbs: 2

Summons a Lifelike Image that's Sustainable for 4 turns to drag all enemies in a picturesque realm, then inflicts Ink-chased on 1 non-summoned enemy entity.
As long as Lifelike Image is on the battlefield, the skill is changed to Blossom Rain, with its skill level equal to the skill level of Picturesque Life.

  • Lv.2: Increases Lifelike lmage's transferred damage to 15%.
  • Lv.3: As long as Lifelike Image is on the battlefield, all allies deal 10% more AoE damage.
  • Lv.4: Increases Lifelike lmage's transferred damage to 20%.
  • Lv.5: As long as Lifelike Image is on the battlefield, AoE damage boost to all allies to 20%.

Ink-chased
[Genera|, Mark]Whenever another enemy takes non-transferred damage, Lifelike Image deals transferred damage to the target equal to 10% of non-transferred damage.

Lifelike Image
Has immunity to damage, debuffs, and controlling effects. Unable to take action. Damage dealt from a single strike won't exceed 1,200% of Virtuoso Hana's max HP.

Sustainable
Represents the turns a status or a mark lasts is counted by its source rather than its target.

Transferred damage
Damage type. Does not land critical hits. Does not trigger the effect of Soul Edge. Transferred by some shikigami skills, including Shouzu's Water Circuit.

Blossom Rain Heal Orbs: 0
Can be used on a non-summoned ally entity other than herself. Heals their HP by 8% of her max HP. The same effect will then bounce to 2 allies, prioritizing on the allies lowest HP proportion.
If the healing lands a critical hit, Lifelike Image deals damage to the enemy with the Ink-chased mark she inflicted equal to 20% of the healing amount as transferred damage. If the enemy is a monster, damage equal to 100% of the healing amount will be dealt as transferred damage instead.
  • Lv.2: Increases the healing amount to 10% of her max HP.
  • Lv.3: Increases the healing effect bounced to 3 allies.
  • Lv.4: Increases the healing amount to 12% of her max HP.
  • Lv.5: Increases the healing effect bounced to 4 allies.
