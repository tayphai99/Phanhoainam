Heartseeker Momiji

Skill 1: Crow Feather
Damage Orbs: 0

Illuminates 1 enemy with focused maple fire before extinguishing the fire, dealing damage equal to 100% of her ATK. This is changed to Leaf Blade while she's Into the Woods, with its skill level equal to the skill level of Crow Feather.

  • Lv.2: Increases damage to 105%.
  • Lv.3: Increases damage to 110%.
  • Lv.4: Increases damage to 115%.
  • Lv.5: Increases damage to 125%.

Into the Woods
[Buff, Mark] Increases indirect damage taken by 20% and reduces non-indirect damage taken by 20%.

Leaf Blade Damage Orbs: 0
Throws a sharp leaf blade to attack 1 enemy which then bounces once, dealing damage equal to 75% of her ATK on each strike. The leaf blade bounces to the unit with the most Burnt Leaf stacks first.
  • Lv.2: Increases damage to 85%.
  • Lv.3: Increases the bouncing times to 2.
  • Lv.4: Increases damage to 100%.
  • Lv.5: Increases the bouncing times to 3.

Skill 2: Crucible
Special Passive Effective Exclusively

Attacking enemies inflicts a Burnt Leaf stack.

  • Lv.2: Increases Burnt Leaf's ability to increase indirect damage taken to 10%.
  • Lv.3: When an ally is inflicted with a debuff, inflicts a Burnt Leaf stack on the source. Triggers up to once each turn.
  • Lv.4: When the Move Bar of an enemy is raised by at least 30%, inflicts a Burnt Leaf stack on them.
  • Lv.5: Each Burnt Leaf stack also reduces ATK and DEF by 10%. When Heartseeker Momiji is attacked, each stack also reduces the attack's Crit by 20%.

Burnt Leaf
[Debuff, Status] At the start of the turn, inflicts indirect damage equal to 10% of the inflicter's ATK. Each stack also inflicts Poison III for 2 turns and increases indirect damage taken by 5%. At the end of the turn, 1 stack is removed. Can stack up to 5 times. Inflicting Burnt Leaf on a unit with 5 stacks will inflict Red Erosion instead.

Red Erosion
[Debuff, Mark] Reduces Move Bar-raising efficiency by 50%. When receiving an attack from Heartseeker Momiji, also takes indirect damage equal to 80% of her ATK. Lasts 2 turns and cannot be dispelled. If Heartseeker Momiji inflicts it outside her turn, raises her Move Bar by 40%.

Effective Exclusively
If multiple shikigami of the same name are in your lineup, only one of those shikigami's skills will be effective.

Skill 3: Maple Dance
Support Special Orbs: 3

Dances freely and awakens the maple leaves, going Into the Woods for 2 turns and creating a Leaf Buffer for all allies. The skill can only be used while she's not Into the Woods.

  • Lv.2: While she's Into the Woods, her attacks also ignore 200 DEF.
  • Lv.3: Leaf Buffer also increases DEF by 100.
  • Lv.4: While she's Into the Woods, increases her indirect damage taken by 10% and reduces her direct damage taken by 30%.
  • Lv.5: Upper Hand: Uses Maple Dance.

Into the Woods
[Buff, Mark] Increases indirect damage taken by 20% and reduces non-indirect damage taken by 20%.

Leaf Buffer
[Buff, Mark] Lasts 2 turns. When the unit takes damage, inflicts a Burnt Leaf stack on the attacker. Triggers up to once each turn.
