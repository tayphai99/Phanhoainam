Higanbana

Skill 1: Deadly Blooms
Damage Orbs: 0

Uses her crimson petals to attack 1 enemy, dealing damage equal to 100% of her ATK.

  • Lv.2: Increases damage to 105%.
  • Lv.3: Increases damage to 110%.
  • Lv.4: Increases damage to 115%.
  • Lv.5: Increases damage to 125%.

Skill 2: Crimson Flowers
Damage Shield Passive Effective Exclusively

At the turn start of an enemy who's surrounded by flowers, each layer of flowers deals damage to them equal to 39% of her ATK.
Flowers are limited to 3/4/5/6 layers when her HP is at 100%/75%/50%/25%. The flowers decrease by 1 layer on each of her turns to a minimum of 1 layer.
Gains a layer of flowers and a Shield created by Blood-soaked Flowers for each 25% of her HP reduced. Upper Hand: Creates 3 layers of flowers to surround all enemies with.

  • Lv.2: Increases damage of each layer of flowers to 41%.
  • Lv.3: Increases damage of each layer of flowers to 43%.
  • Lv.4: Increases damage of each layer of flowers to 45%.
  • Lv.5: Increases damage of each layer of flowers to 48%.

Effective Exclusively
If multiple shikigami of the same name are in your lineup, only one of those shikigami's skills will be effective.

Shield
Absorbs an amount of damage. Can be dispelled unless otherwise specified.

Upper Hand
Take action at the start of battle.

Skill 3: Blood-soaked Flowers
Shield Special Orbs: 3

Gains 2 layers of flowers.
If her HP is below 75%, also gains a Shield that cannot be dispelled and lasts 2 turns, absorbing damage equal to 20% of her HP lost.

  • Lv.2: Increases damage absorbed by Shield to 22% of HP lost.
  • Lv.3: Increases damage absorbed by Shield to 24% of HP lost.
  • Lv.4: Increases damage absorbed by Shield to 26% of HP lost.
  • Lv.5: Increases damage absorbed by Shield to 28% of HP lost.

Shield
Absorbs an amount of damage. Can be dispelled unless otherwise specified.
