Seawatch Kingyo

Skill 1: River Water
Damage Dispel Orbs: 0

Channels a stream of water to attack 1 enemy, dealing damage equal to 80% of her ATK. Also dispels 1 buff from them.
When an ally takes damage from an enemy, if the damage dealt exceeds 30% of the ally's max HP, inflicts Grudged lasting 2 turns on the source of the damage.

  • Lv.2: Increases damage to 85%.
  • Lv.3: Increases damage to 90%.
  • Lv.4: Increases damage to 95%.
  • Lv.5: Increases damage to 100%.

Grudged
[Debuff, Status] Reduces ATK by 20%. Cannot be stacked.

Skill 2: Ride Wave
Shield Accelerator Passive

At the end of an enemy's turn, gains a layer of Focused and creates a shield to protect herself if she doesn't have one. The shield absorbs damage equal to 80% of her ATK.
Upper Hand: Gains 4 layers of Focused.

  • This skill cannot be leveled up.

Focused
[General, Mark] Seawatch Kingyo's special mechanism. Stacks up to 8 times. When the stacks max out, all stacks are removed to remove all controlling effects on her, inflicts River's Binding lasting 2 turns on all enemies, and activates a Fishtail Embrace that lasts 1 turn. If she already has a Fishtail Embrace, raises the Move Bar of all allies by 25% instead.

River's Binding
[Debuff, Mark] When the unit receives a single-target Move Bar-raising effect, focuses spiritual fish to reverse the flow, reducing their SPD by 40% at the end of their turn.

Upper Hand
Take action at the start of battle.

Skill 3: Goldfish's Embrace
Damage Heal Accelerator Orbs: 3

Raises the Move Bar of all allies by 10% and creates a Fishtail Embrace (Effective Exclusively) that lasts for 1 turn. As long as it's active, if she's not Unable to Take Actions, heals the HP of an ally by 50% of her own ATK at the end of their turn. Also, randomly inflicts Indirect Damage equal to 50% of her ATK on an enemy. Enemies with Grudged are prioritized, and the damage is increased to 100% of her ATK for them. All Grudged debuffs on them are consumed afterward.

  • Lv.2: Increases the damage to targets with Grudged to 300%.
  • Lv.3: Fishtail Embrace additional effect: Reduces non-critical damage to allies by 30%.
  • Lv.4: Increases her healing effects on allies with HP equal or lower than 50% to 200%.
  • Lv.5: Reduces orbs required by 1.

Grudged
[Debuff, Status] Reduces ATK by 20%. Cannot be stacked.

Effective Exclusively
If multiple shikigami of the same name are in your lineup, only one of those shikigami's skills will be effective.

Unable to Take Actions
Units that are Banished or inflicted with controlling effects, including Freeze, Sleep, Daze, Morph, Deep Freeze, Shadowbound, and Frostbound are unable to take actions.

Indirect Damage
Damage type. Does not trigger soul effects, cannot be shared, and is guaranteed to land critical hits on enemies with 0 DEF.
