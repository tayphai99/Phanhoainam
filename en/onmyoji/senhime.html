Senhime

Skill 1: Thousand Tides
Damage Orbs: 0

Sends splashing waves to attack 1 enemy, dealing damage equal to 100% of her ATK.

  • Lv.2: Increases damage to 105%.
  • Lv.3: Increases damage to 110%.
  • Lv.4: Increases damage to 120%.
  • Lv.5: Increases damage to 125%. If Murex Halberd is present, it gains 1 Sound of the Tides stack.

Skill 2: Tidal Dream
Damage Orbs: 2

Rides the waves. At the end of a non-summoned entity enemy's turn, raises the Move Bar of Senhime by 10%.
Deals damage to 1 enemy equal to 172% of her ATK. If Murex Halberd is on the battlefield, also inflicts Tidal Dream on them for 1 turn.

  • Lv.2: Increases damage to 185%.
  • Lv.3: Increases damage to 198%.
  • Lv.4: Increases damage to 211%.
  • Lv.5: If the enemy with Tidal Dream has 0 orbs after taking an action, inflicts Deep Freeze on them for 1 turn.

Tidal Dream
[Debuff, Mark] Consumed after taking an action. Removes 3 orbs. No effect on monsters.

Skill 3: Tame the Tides
Damage Heal Support Special Orbs: 0

Summons Murex Halberd to receive blessings from the tides.

  • Lv.2: When Murex Halberd is on the battlefield, Senhime and Murex Halberd take 30% less damage.
  • Lv.3: When Murex Halberd is on the battlefield, increases Murex Halberd's chance of gaining a Sound of the Tides stack each time an ally spends an orb to 100%.
  • Lv.4: Senhime gains immunity to controlling effects and Banish as long as she's wielding Murex Halberd in her hands.
  • Lv.5: When Murex Halberd is summoned, it immediately gains 3 Sound of the Tides stacks.

Sound of the Tides
[Buff, Mark] Each time Murex Halberd gains 7 Sound of the Tides stacks, you gain 3 orbs, and the damage of all allies is permanently increased by 15%, up to 5 times.

Murex Halberd
Immune to debuffs and controlling effects. Cannot take actions. DEFs equal to 100% of Senhime's DEF. HP equals to 550% of Senhime's ATK.
When it's on the battlefield, it recovers the HP of an ally by 30% of the damage taken. Murex Halberd has a 50% chance of gaining a Sound of the Tides stack when an ally spends an orb.

Recover
Different from Healing. Unaffected by Heal Down effects. Does not trigger effects related to healing, and cannot land Crits.
