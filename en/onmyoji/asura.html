Asura

Skill 1: Annihilation Flames
Damage Special Orbs: 0

Wields Bonelash to attack 1 enemy, dealing damage equal to 100% of his ATK.
When Asura KO's the enemy, he inflicts Annihilation on an enemy and Mighty Crush changes to Endless Slaughter.

  • Lv.2: Increases damage to 105%.
  • Lv.3: Increases damage to 110%.
  • Lv.4: Increases damage to 115%.
  • Lv.5: Increases damage to 125%.

Annihilation
[General, Mark] Reduces ATK by 50% indefinitely.

Skill 2: Mighty Crush
Special Orbs: 0

At the start of battle and the start of his turn, he gains 9 points of Sanity. He gains immunity to Controlling Effect and Banish if he doesn't have 9 points of Sanity. At the start of each attack, consumes an amount of Sanity equal to the number of units he's attacking.

  • Lv.2: Reduces Sanity's damage suppressing effect to 8%.
  • Lv.3: Reduces Sanity's damage suppressing effect to 7%.
  • Lv.4: Reduces Sanity's damage suppressing effect to 6%.
  • Lv.5: Each point of Sanity he has lost reduces his damage taken by 6%.

Sanity
[General, Mark] Max 9 points. Each point of Sanity Asura restored recovers his HP by 8% of his max HP. Asura is also suppressed by Sanity. Each point of Sanity he has reduces his damage dealt by 9%.

Controlling Effect
Freeze, Sleep, Confuse, Taunt, Silence, Bound, Morph, Daze, Provocation, Deep Freeze, Shadowbound, and Frostbound are controlling effects. The last six cannot be dispelled. Frostbound cannot be removed.

Banish
Banished target is unable to take actions, they cannot be selected, their Move Bar is locked, their passive skills and Souls equipped won't take effect, they are immune to damage, healing effects, buffs and debuffs.

Recover
Different from Healing. Unaffected by Heal Down effects. Does not trigger effects related to healing, and cannot land Crits.

Endless Slaughter Damage Orbs: 0
Gains immunity to controlling effects and Banish. Uses Endless Slaughter automatically at the start of his turn. At the end of another non-summoned entity ally's turn, recovers 1 point of Sanity for Asura. If you have Taishakuten, then at the end of Taishakuten's turn, recovers an additional 3 points of Sanity for Asura.
Using the skill if he doesn't have 9 points of Sanity, summons giant swords to attack 1 random unit other than himself, dealing damage equal to 263% of his ATK. Then rains down swords to attack all other units, dealing damage equal to 113% of his ATK; if he has 9 points of Sanity, the skill is changed back to Mighty Crush.

Skill 3: Inferno Slaughter
Damage Orbs: 3

Plunges into the enemy lineup and summons forth a giant hand to attack all enemies, dealing damage equal to 290% of his ATK.
Then inflicts a follow-up attack on the enemy until his Sanity points are fully consumed, dealing damage equal to 54% of his ATK on each attack.

  • Lv.2: Increases the damage to all enemies to 305%.
  • Lv.3: Increases the damage to all enemies to 315%.
  • Lv.4: Increases the follow-up damage to 63%.
  • Lv.5: If the target is KO'd and he still has Sanity left, he will then attack another enemy with the lowest HP.
