Lord Arakawa

Skill 1: Roaming Fish
Damage Orbs: 0

Forms water into a fish to assault 1 enemy, dealing damage equal to 100% of his ATK.

  • Lv.2: Increases damage to 105%.
  • Lv.3: Increases damage to 110%.
  • Lv.4: Increases damage to 115%.
  • Lv.5: Increases damage to 125%.

Skill 2: Empowering Flow
Passive

Deals an extra 20% damage when launching a critical hit, but inflicts Empowering Flow lasting 1 turn on himself.

  • This skill cannot be leveled up.

Empowering Flow
[Debuff, Status] Reduces DEF by 20%. Whenever he deals damage, if the target has higher DEF, the damage dealt ignores 50% of the DEF difference.

Skill 3: Swallow
Damage Dispel Orbs: 3

Forms water into a shoal of fish to inflict a 2-strike attack on 1 enemy, dealing damage equal to 53% of his ATK and has a 100% Base Chance of inflicting Isolation lasting 2 turns on them on the first strike, and dealing damage equal to 211% of his ATK on the second strike.

  • Lv.2: Increases damage of the second stage to 226%.
  • Lv.3: Increases damage of the second stage to 241%.
  • Lv.4: Increases damage of the second stage to 256%.
  • Lv.5: Increases damage of the second stage to 270%.

Base Chance
Chance is affected by Effect HIT.

Isolation
Damage taken cannot be shared or redirected by other effects, including Hakuzosu's skill Shadowing Shelter, Shouzu's skill Water Circuit and Soul Edge's effect among others.
