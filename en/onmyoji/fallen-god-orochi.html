Fallen God Orochi

Skill 1: Soul's Punishment
Damage Orbs: 0

Gazes at the evil in the depth of the soul and makes a verdict, dealing damage equal to 100% of his ATK to 1 enemy.

  • Lv.2: Increases damage to 105%.
  • Lv.3: Increases damage to 110%.
  • Lv.4: Increases damage to 115%.
  • Lv.5: Increases damage to 125%.

Skill 2: Corrupted Force
Special Orbs: 0 Effective Exclusively

He's suppressed by a total of 6 Divine Swords: Ame-no-Habakiri.

Using the skill turns himself into The Serpent Lord, storing 6% of the starting ATK of all other shikigami allies in 1 Ame-no-Habakiri to serve as the energy required to break the seal of Ame-no-Habakiri. When he's KO'd, all stored ATK will be returned to its respective owners. If the skill is used in succession, its orb cost increases by 1 orb each time.

  • Lv.2: Increases The Serpent Lord's HP to 380% of Fallen God Orochi's ATK.
  • Lv.3: Reduces The Serpent Lord's AoE damage taken by 30%.
  • Lv.4: When The Serpent Lord is KO'd, increases his SPD by 100 for 1 turn.
  • Lv.5: Upper Hand: Uses Corrupted Force.

Effective Exclusively
If multiple shikigami of the same name are in your lineup, only one of those shikigami's skills will be effective.

The Serpent Lord
His max HP cannot be changed. His HP is equal to 200% of Fallen God Orochi's ATK. He has immunity to debuffs and Banish. When The Serpent Lord removes the suppression of an Ame-no-Habakiri with a Trial Ritual or he takes lethal damage, his original form returns to the battlefield.

Corrupted Sword
Serves as an offering to awaken the sealed power of the Evil God. Inherits the stats of the original shikigami, except its HP is equal to 310% of Fallen God Orochi's ATK. On its action turn, recovers its HP by 20%, grants 1 orb, and raises Fallen God Orochi's Move Bar by 15%. Each sword reduces Fallen God Orochi's damage taken by 20%.

Final Judgment
While the field is active, Fallen God Orochi gains immunity to debuffs and Banish.

Offering
Regarded as KO'd. Sacrificed allies don't trigger revival effects and cannot be revived.

Upper Hand
Take action at the start of battle.

Serpent's Devouring Damage Orbs: 0
Can only use the skill when he's Fallen God Orochi. Removes the seal of the 6th Ame-no-Habakiri and creates the Final Judgment field, taking the onmyoji's spot for the remainder of the battle. Gains a new turn, changes Trial Ritual to Final Judgment, and summons a Corrupted Sword on the original spot. Also sacrifices all remaining non-summoned ally entities, turning them into Corrupted Swords and taking an amount of ATK equal to 6% of their starting ATK.

Skill 3: Trial Ritual
Damage Support Orbs: 3

Only The Serpent Lord can use the skill.
Active skill: Removes the suppression of 1 Ame-no-Habakiri to attack all enemies, dealing damage equal to 188% of his ATK and inflicting Corrupted to another shikigami ally.
If a total of 5 Ame-no-Habakiri have been removed, sets the powerful evil force free and changes Corrupted Force to Serpent's Devouring.

  • Lv.2: When using the skill, for each 20% HP The Serpent Lord has, recovers his HP by 12%
  • Lv.3: When using the skill, for each 20% HP The Serpent Lord has, raises his Move Bar by 8%
  • Lv.4: When using the skill, for each 20% HP The Serpent Lord has, increases this time's damage multiplier by 25%
  • Lv.5: When using the skill, withdraws the allies' stored ATK (max 100% of his own starting ATK).

Corrupted
[General, Mark] Increases SPD by 40%. At the beginning of the turn, corrupts 24% of the current HP. Having HP corrupted counts as losing HP and removes Sleep. When the unit is KO'd, raises Fallen God Orochi's Move Bar by 40% and turns the unit to a Corrupted Sword. If the number of Ame-no-Habakiri removed is fewer than 5, removes 1 Ame-no-Habakiri immediately.

The Serpent Lord
His max HP cannot be changed. His HP is equal to 200% of Fallen God Orochi's ATK. He has immunity to debuffs and Banish. When The Serpent Lord removes the suppression of an Ame-no-Habakiri with a Trial Ritual or he takes lethal damage, his original form returns to the battlefield.

Recover
Different from Healing. Unaffected by Heal Down effects. Does not trigger effects related to healing, and cannot land Crits.

Final Judgment Damage Orbs: 3
Attacks all enemies, dealing damage equal to 313% of his ATK. Then inflicts 4 follow-up strikes on random enemies, dealing damage equal to 128% of his ATK on each strike.
