Yamalord Enma

Skill 1: Verdict on the Dead
Damage Orbs: 0

Unleashes inferno punishment on 1 enemy, dealing damage equal to 100% of her ATK.

  • Lv.2: Increases damage to 105%.
  • Lv.3: Increases damage to 110%.
  • Lv.4: Increases damage to 115%.
  • Lv.5: Increases damage to 125% and grants a 60% Base Chance of inflicting a Eyes of Enma stack.

Eyes of Enma
[Debuff, Status]When stacked 3 times, consumes all stacks and converts into Evil's Imprisonment.

Evil's Imprisonment
[Debuff, Mark] lf a skill is not in cooldown, imposes an immediate 1-turn cooldown on the skill. Ineffective on monsters. Each Evil's Imprisonment on an enemy reduces Yamalord Enma's SPD by 5%.

Skill 2: Book of Reincarnation
Dispel Accelerator Special Passive Effective Exclusively

At the end of her turn, records 40% of the total starting HP of all non-summoned ally entities in Book of Reincarnation.

When the HP recorded in Book of Reincarnation is reduced to 0 or a non summoned ally entity is KO'd, as long as she's not under any Controlling Effect or is Banished, uses Requiem Realm on the source of the damage free of orb cost. If she's under any Controlling Effect, Removes all the Controlling Effects on her and raises her Move Bar by 30% (can be triggered only up to 5 times each round.)

  • Lv.2: Records 37% of all allies' HP.
  • Lv.3: Records 34% of all allies' HP.
  • Lv.4: Records 30% of all allies' HP.
  • Lv.5: At the end of an enemy's turn, if no enemy is in Evil's Imprisonment, there's a 60% base chance of inflicting an Eyes of Enma stack on the enemy.

Eyes of Enma
[Debuff, Status]When stacked 3 times, consumes all stacks and converts into Evil's Imprisonment.

Evil's Imprisonment
[Debuff, Mark]lf a skill is not in cooldown, imposes an immediate 1-turn cooldown on the skill. Ineffective on monsters. Each Evil's Imprisonment on an enemy reduces Yamalord Enma's SPD by 5%.

Effective Exclusively
If multiple shikigami of the same name are in your lineup, only one of those shikigami's skills will be effective.

Book of Reincarnation
At the end of her turn, records 40% of the total starting HP of all non-summoned ally entities. When a non-summoned ally entity takes enemy damage, an equal amount of HP is deducted from the Book of Reincarnation.

Controlling Effect
Freeze, Sleep, Confuse, Taunt, Silence, Bound, Morph, Daze, Provocation, Deep Freeze, Shadowbound, and Frostbound are controlling effects. The last six cannot be dispelled. Frostbound cannot be removed.

Banish
Banished target is unable to take actions, they cannot be selected, their Move Bar is locked, their passive skills and Souls equipped won't take effect, they are immune to damage, healing effects, buffs and debuffs.

Remove
Removes status and effects that cannot be removed.

Skill 3: Requiem Realm
Damage Control Special Orbs: 3

Removes the names of the dead souls, granting them a painless peace.

Launches an attack on 1 enemy, dealing damage equal to 232% of her ATK, with a 70% Base Chance of inflicting Morph lasting 1 turn on them. (Effective Exclusively) There's a 60% Base Chance of inflicting 2 Eyes of Enma stacks on all enemies.

  • Lv.2: Increases damage to 240%.
  • Lv.3: Increases damage to 248%.
  • Lv.4: Increases damage to 256%.
  • Lv.5: If she fails to inflict Morph, there's a 100% base chance of inflicting a Eyes of Enma stack.

Eyes of Enma
[Debuff, Status]When stacked 3 times, consumes all stacks and converts into Evil's Imprisonment.

Evil's Imprisonment
[Debuff, Mark]lf a skill is not in cooldown, imposes an immediate 1-turn cooldown on the skill. Ineffective on monsters. Each Evil's Imprisonment on an enemy reduces Yamalord Enma's SPD by 5%.

Base Chance
Chance is affected by Effect HIT.

Morph
Cannot be dispelled. Prevents shikigami passive skills from taking effect. Morphed targets are unable to take actions.

Effective Exclusively
If multiple shikigami of the same name are in your lineup, only one of those shikigami's skills will be effective.
