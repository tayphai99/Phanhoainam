Kidoumaru

Skill 1: Arrival of Death
Damage Debuff Orbs: 0 Effective Exclusively

Unleashes his chains to attack 1 enemy twice, dealing damage equal to 50% of his ATK on each attack. Also there is a 30% chance of inflicting Bone Look on the enemy. If no enemies have Bone Lock, the chance is increased to 100%.

  • Lv.2: Increases damage to 53%.
  • Lv.3: Increases damage to 56%.
  • Lv.4: Increases damage to 59%.
  • Lv.5: Attacking a unit with Bone Lock gains him a layer of Demon Madness at the end of the turn.

Effective Exclusively
If multiple shikigami of the same name are in your lineup, only one of those shikigami's skills will be effective.

Skill 2: Demon Outfit
Shield Special Passive Effective Exclusively

Kidomaru enters the Asura state when he has 3 layers of Demon Madness.
When he's in the Asura state, he uses Soul Hunter on all enemies at the end of an enemy's turn.

  • Lv.2: Increases his shield's damage absorbed 133%.
  • Lv.3: Increases his shield's damage absorbed 146%.
  • Lv.4: Increases his shield's damage absorbed 160%.
  • Lv.5: When his Asura state ends, each Bone Lock removed reduces Asura Chains' orb cost by 1.

Asura
[Buff, Mark] Gains a shield that can absorb damage equal to 120% of his ATK, and immunity to controlling effects.
Asura state ends when Soul Hunter is used 3 times or the shield fails, clearing all layers of Demon Madness on him and all Bone Locks on enemies.
At the start of Kidomaru's turn, if Asura state has not been removed, Kidomaru will first use up his remaining Soul Hunter attempts.

Demon Madness
[Buff, Mark] Increases SPD by 20 and damage by 30%. Stackable up to 3 layers.

Effective Exclusively
If multiple shikigami of the same name are in your lineup, only one of those shikigami's skills will be effective.

Soul Hunter Damage Heal Orbs: 0
Launches an attack on all enemies, dealing damage equal to 32% of his ATK. Damage dealt by Soul Hunter won't trigger target's Soul effects.
The skill level is equal to the skill level of Demon Outfit.
  • Lv.2: Increases damage to 34%.
  • Lv.3: Increases damage to 36%.
  • Lv.4: Increases damage to 40%.
  • Lv.5: When his Asura state ends, each Bone Lock removed recovers Kidomaru's HP by 25% of his max HP.

Skill 3: Asura Chains
Damage Special Debuff Orbs: 3 Effective Exclusively

Kidomaru launches a 3-strike attack on 1 enemy, dealing damage equal to 70% of his ATK and inflicting the Bone Lock effect on them on each strike. Kidomaru then becomes Hidden Secret and gains a layer of Demon Madness at the end of the turn. If Kidomaru is already in the Hidden Secret state, he cannot becomes Hidden Secret again.

  • Lv.2: Increases damage to 75%.
  • Lv.3: Increases damage to 80%.
  • Lv.4: Increases damage to 90%.
  • Lv.5: Upper Hand: Becomes Hidden Secret.

Bone Lock
[Debuff, Mark] Reduces target's SPD by 20 and their ATK by 30%, and prevents their Move Bar from being altered. Lasts 2 turns. When Kidomaru deals damage, additional damage equal to 60% of his ATK is inflicted on targets with Bone Lock. This resets Bone Lock turns.

Hidden Secret
[Buff, Mark] Kidomaru's special state. Enemy skills will prioritize units other than Kidomaru's. Lasts 3 turns. Kidomaru takes 20% less damage and his attack inflicts additional true damage equal to 33% of his ATK when he's Hidden. Kidomaru's Hidden state ends after he launches an attack.

Demon Madness
[Buff, Mark] Increases SPD by 20 and damage by 30%. Stackable up to 3 layers.

Effective Exclusively
If multiple shikigami of the same name are in your lineup, only one of those shikigami's skills will be effective.

Upper Hand
Take action at the start of battle.
