Worldly Aoandon

Skill 1: Floating Flame
Damage Debuff Orbs: 0

Forms a butterfly out of scorching blue flame to ignite 1 enemy, dealing damage equal to 100% of her ATK and inflicting Light Drain lasting 2 turn(s) on the enemy.

  • Lv.2: Increases damage to 105%.
  • Lv.3: Increases damage to 110%.
  • Lv.4: Increases damage to 115%.
  • Lv.5: Increases damage to 125%.

Light Drain
[Debuff, Mark] Using skills the first time costs an extra 1 orb. Lasts 2 turns. Ineffective on monsters and summoned entities.

Skill 2: Lights On
Special passive Effective Exclusively

Aoandon re-writes the ending of all lives, gaining Unextinguished indefinitely.
For a unit with Light Drain, inflicting Light Drain on them again removes 2 orbs from them; Worldly Aoandon collects the removed orbs.
She collects all the orbs you have gained. Whenever she collects 30 orbs, she immediately uses Lantern of Death free of orbs. Whenever she collects 100 orbs, she gains the additional skill Final Chapter.

  • Lv.2: Collects all your overflow orbs.
  • Lv.3: Collects 1 orb when an ally uses a skill.
  • Lv.4: Collects all the orbs spent on using skills by units with Light Drain.
  • Lv.5: When you gain orbs from the Orb Bar, you gain 1 additional orb. At the end of her turn, Orb Bar fills up by 1 extra slot.

Unextinguished
[General, Mark] She gains 1 stack for each orb she collects. Max 100 stacks. The stacks don't reset when she's revived after being KO'd. Each stack increases the ATK and DEF of all allies by 1%.

Light Drain
[Debuff, Mark] Using skills the first time costs an extra 1 orb. Lasts 2 turns. Ineffective on monsters and summoned entities.

Effective Exclusively
If multiple shikigami of the same name are in your lineup, only one of those shikigami's skills will be effective.

Final Chapter Damage Orbs: 3
Consumes all the orbs collected to launch a 4-strike attack on all enemies, dealing damage equal to 1,200% of her ATK with each strike. Damage is shared equally among all enemies.

Skill 3: Lantern of Death
Damage Debuff Orbs: 3

The Lantern of Death falls, heralding impending doom.
Chooses 1 enemy then attacks all enemies. Inflicts damage on the chosen enemy equal to 211% of her ATK and Light Drain lasting 2 turns on them.
Then inflicts damage on every other enemy equal to 105% of her ATK. Chooses 2 enemies at random and inflicts Light Drain lasting 2 turn(s) on them, prioritizing the ones without Light Drain.

  • Lv.2: Increases damage dealt to the target enemy to 216% and to all other enemies to 110%.
  • Lv.3: Increases damage dealt to the target enemy to 221% and to all other enemies to 115%.
  • Lv.4: Increases damage dealt to the target enemy to 226% and to all other enemies to 120%.
  • Lv.5: Increases damage dealt to the target enemy to 231% and to all other enemies to 125%.

Light Drain
[Debuff, Mark] Using skills the first time costs an extra 1 orb. Lasts 2 turns. Ineffective on monsters and summoned entities.
