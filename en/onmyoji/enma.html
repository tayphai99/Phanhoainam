Enma

Skill 1: Demon Mask
Damage Orbs: 0

Summons a demon mask to strike 1 enemy, dealing damage equal to 100% of her ATK.

  • Lv.2: Increases damage to 105%.
  • Lv.3: Increases damage to 110%.
  • Lv.4: Increases damage to 115%.
  • Lv.5: Increases damage to 125%.

Skill 2: Soul Plunder
DamageControl Passive

Enma gains Ghost Rider.
Summons 1 White Ghost when an enemy is KO'd. The White Ghost sacrifices itself to attack all enemies on its next action, dealing damage equal to 100% of her ATK. The damage dealt won't trigger the Soul-set effects and passive skills of all enemies.
The White Ghost inherits 10% of Enma's HP and 50% of her ATK. The enemy whose spot is occupied by the White Ghost cannot be revived.
Spirit Crush has a 50% Base Chance of turning a Morphed enemy into a Black Ghost for 1 turn.

  • Lv.2: Increases Base Chance of inflicting Morph to 55%.
  • Lv.3: Increases Base Chance of inflicting Morph to 60%.
  • Lv.4: Increases Base Chance of inflicting Morph to 65%.
  • Lv.5: Increases Base Chance of inflicting Morph to 70%.

Ghost Rider
[Buff, Mark] lf there's a ghost on the enemy's side, Enma gains 50% damage reduction. Otherwise, her damage is raised by 50%.

Base Chance
Chance is affected by Effect HIT.

Morph
Cannot be dispelled. Prevents shikigami passive skills from taking effect. Morphed targets are unable to take actions.

Damage Control Orbs: 3

  • Lv.2: Increases damage to 222%.
  • Lv.3: Increases damage to 233%.
  • Lv.4: Increases damage to 244%.
  • Lv.5: Increases damage to 255%.

Base Chance
Chance is affected by Effect HIT.

Silence
Prevents using skills.
