Shokurei

Skill 1: Stir Wok
Damage Orbs: 0

Stirs his divine wok to attack enemies, dealing damage equal to 100% of his ATK.

  • Lv.2: Increases damage to 105%.
  • Lv.3: Increases damage to 110%.
  • Lv.4: Increases damage to 115%.
  • Lv.5: Increases damage to 125%. Using the skill outside the turn also burns the enemy, inflicting 20% Vulnerability on them for 2 turns.

Skill 2: Heat Wave
Support Passive Effective Exclusively

Inspires all allies with his passion and cuisine, increasing their damage dealt outside the turn by 30%.

  • Lv.2: Boosts damage by 40% on others' turns.
  • Lv.3: The ally with the highest starting Crit DMG gains an additional 30% chance of Assisting.
  • Lv.4: Boosts damage by 50% on others' turns.
  • Lv.5: When the ally with the highest starting Crit DMG targets an enemy in their turn, Shokurei will also launch a normal attack.

Effective Exclusively
If multiple shikigami of the same name are in your lineup, only one of those shikigami's skills will be effective.

Skill 3: Dream Cuisine
Heal Support Special Orbs: 3

Prepares a Dream Cuisine for 1 ally and serves them a Meal for 1 turn. At the turn and of their turn, the ally will finish their food and Meal and have a Full Stomach.
Cannot be used on self or allies having a Meal.

  • Lv.2: Reduces orbs required by 1.
  • Lv.3: When an ally finishes their food, grants Shelter for 1 turn.
  • Lv.4: When an ally finishes their food, recovers the HP of all allies by 78% of Shokurei's ATK.
  • Lv.5: For each enemy on the battlefield, Feast inflicts additional True Damage equal to 66% of Shokurei's ATK.

Meal
[General, Mark] lndulges in gourmet cuisine, with no interest in doing battle. Reduces damage dealt on own turns by 30%. Also reduces damage taken by 40%.

Full Stomach
[General, Mark] l'm full and ready to work! Activates Feast after 3 turns (either ally's or enemy's) if he's not unable to take action, dealing damage to all enemies.

Shelter
Negates all controlling effects and Banish inflicted by a single skill.

Recover
Different from Healing. Unaffected by Heal Down effects. Does not trigger effects related to healing, and cannot land Crits.

Feast
Inflicts damage evenly on all enemies equal to 158% of the damage dealt by the ally who has had a Meal on their turn. The damage inflicted ignores DEF, doesn't land critical hit, and doesn't trigger the Soul effects and passive skills for carriers or enemies. It is only affected by the damage boost on Shokurei. (Damage dealt on a single target won't exceed 3,000% of the starting total ATK of all allied shikigami)
