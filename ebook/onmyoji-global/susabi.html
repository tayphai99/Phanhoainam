Susabi

Kỹ năng 1: Star Beam
Sát Thương Lửa: 0

Releases a beam of starlight to strike 1 enemy, dealing damage equal to 100% of his ATK and inflicting a Star Mark.

  • Lv.2: Tăng sát thương lên 105%.
  • Lv.3: Tăng sát thương lên 110%.
  • Lv.4: Tăng sát thương lên 115%.
  • Lv.5: Tăng sát thương lên 125%.

Star Mark
[General, Mark] Max 5 stacks. When the stacks maxed out, if Susabi is not under controlling effects, consumes all stacks to use a Scourge: Star on the enemy free of orb cost.

Kỹ năng 2: Stellar Field
Đặc Biệt Lửa: 2 Hiệu Quả Duy Nhất

At the start of his turn, has a 30% chance of forming an attack-assisting field lasting 1 turn. While the field is active, at the start of Susabi's turn, dispels 1 debuff or Controlling Effect on him; Susabi has a 50% chance of Co-oping an ally when the ally uses a normal attack.
Active skill: Forms an attack-assisting field or extends its duration by 1 turn and inflicts 3 Star Mark stacks on 1 enemy.

  • Lv.2: Increases the chance of forming the field to 50%.
  • Lv.3: Increases the chance of forming the field to 60%.
  • Lv.4: Chance of forming a field is 100% if 4 or more orbs are available.
  • Lv.5: While the field is active, using the skill additionally raises the Move Bar of all allies by 25%.

Star Mark
[General, Mark] Max 5 stacks. When the stacks maxed out, if Susabi is not under controlling effects, consumes all stacks to use a Scourge: Star on the enemy free of orb cost.

Hiệu Quả Duy Nhất
Nếu có nhiều thức thần cùng tên trong đội hình của bạn, chỉ một trong các kỹ năng của thức thần đó sẽ có hiệu lực.

Hiệu Ứng Kiểm Soát
Freeze, Sleep, Confuse, Taunt, Silence, Bound, Morph, Daze, Provocation, Deep Freeze, Shadowbound, and Frostbound are controlling effects. The last six cannot be dispelled. Frostbound cannot be removed.

Co-op
When an ally uses a normal attack to attack an enemy, they use a normal attack to attack the same enemy.

Kỹ năng 3: Scourge: Star
Sát Thương Lửa: 3

Summons comets to inflict 3 strikes on 1 enemy, dealing damage equal to 120% of his ATK for the first strike. Reduces his damage by 10% for each subsequent strike. If the enemy is not KO'd, inflicts a Star Mark on them.

  • Lv.2: Tăng sát thương lên 135% and enhances Scourge: Moon.
  • Lv.3: Tăng sát thương lên 150% and enhances Scourge: Moon.
  • Lv.4: If the enemy is not KO'd, inflicts a Star Mark on them and enhances Scourge: Moon.
  • Lv.5: If the enemy is a non-summoned entity and is KO'd, remaining comets inflict damage to all enemies instead and enhance Scourge: Moon.
