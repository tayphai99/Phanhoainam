Ryomen

Kỹ năng 1: Random Element
Sát Thương Giảm Tốc Tăng Tốc Lửa: 0

Uses one of his faces to strike 1 enemy, dealing damage equal to 100% of their ATK if Raijin strikes or dealing damage equal to 25% of their ATK and inflicting Wind Fury (deals Indirect Damage equal to 125% of their ATK) lasting 1 turn on the enemy if Fujin strikes.

  • Lv.2: Increases Raijin's damage to 105%, Fujin's damage to 27%, and indirect damage to 132%.
  • Lv.3: Increases Raijin's damage to 110%, Fujin's damage to 28%, and indirect damage to 138%.
  • Lv.4: Increases Raijin's damage to 115%, Fujin's damage to 29%, and indirect damage to 144%.
  • Lv.5: Increases Raijin's damage to 125%, Fujin's damage to 32%, and indirect damage to 157%.

Wind Fury
[Debuff, Status] Inflicts Indirect Damage at the end of the turn.

Indirect Damage
Damage type. Does not trigger soul effects, cannot be shared, and is guaranteed to land critical hits on enemies with 0 DEF.

Kỹ năng 2: Eyes of Wrath
Hiệu Ứng Bất Lợi Giảm Tốc Tăng Tốc Bị Động

When they attack, Raijin has a 100% Tỷ Lệ Cơ Bản of inflicting Eyes of Wrath: Thunder on the target and Fujin has a 100% Base Chance of inflicting Eyes of Wrath: Wind on the target. Lasts 2 turns.

  • Lv.2: Increases DEF and ATK reduction to 12%.
  • Lv.3: Increases DEF and ATK reduction to 14%.
  • Lv.4: Increases DEF and ATK reduction to 16%.
  • Lv.5: Raijin lowers the Move Bar of all enemies with lower ATK by 8% when he deals damage. Fujin raises Ryomen's Move Bar by 8% when he deals indirect damage to enemies with lower DEF.

Eyes of Wrath: Thunder
[Debuff, Status] Reduces DEF by 10%.

Eyes of Wrath: Wind
[Debuff, Status] Reduces ATK by 10%.

Tỷ Lệ Cơ Bản
Tỷ lệ bị ảnh hưởng bởi Hiệu Ứng Chính Xác (Effect HIT).

Kỹ năng 3: Divine Combo
Sát Thương Giảm Tốc Tăng Tốc Lửa: 3

Invades the enemy lineup and uses one of his faces by random to attack all enemies 3 times, dealing damage equal to 44% of their ATK each time if Raijin strikes or dealing damage equal to 1 1% of their ATK each time and inflicting Vortex Assault (deals Indirect Damage equal to 55% of their ATK) lasting 1 turn on them if Fujin strikes.

  • Lv.2: Increases Raijin's damage to 47%, Fujin's damage to 12%, and indirect damage to 59%.
  • Lv.3: Increases Raijin's damage to 49%, Fujin's damage to 13%, and indirect damage to 61%.
  • Lv.4: Increases Raijin's damage to 51%, Fujin's damage to 14%, and indirect damage to 64%.
  • Lv.5: Increases Raijin's damage to 55%, Fujin's damage to 15%, and indirect damage to 69%.

Vortex Assault
[Debuff, Status] Inflicts Indirect Damage at the end of the turn.

Indirect Damage
Damage type. Does not trigger soul effects, cannot be shared, and is guaranteed to land critical hits on enemies with 0 DEF.
