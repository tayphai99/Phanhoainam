Demoniac Shuten Doji

Kỹ năng 1: Blazing Fire
Sát Thương Lửa: 0

Commands a ball of flame to attack 1 enemy, dealing damage equal to 100% of his ATK.
When he's in Demoniac state, this is changed to Torch the Sky, with its skill level equal to the skill level of Blazing Fire.

  • Lv.2: Tăng sát thương lên 105%.
  • Lv.3: Tăng sát thương lên 110%.
  • Lv.4: Tăng sát thương lên 115%.
  • Lv.5: Tăng sát thương lên 125%.
Torch the Sky Sát Thương Lửa: 0
Calls forth a huge blazing comet to smash 1 enemy, dealing damage equal to 196% of his ATK. Also inflicts splash damage on other enemies, dealing True Damage equal to 20% of the damage the first target takes. Splash damage won't trigger his own Soul effects.
  • Lv.2: Tăng sát thương lên 206% and splash damage proportion to 25%.
  • Lv.3: Tăng sát thương lên 216% and splash damage proportion to 30%.
  • Lv.4: Tăng sát thương lên 226% and splash damage proportion to 35%.
  • Lv.5: Tăng sát thương lên 245% and splash damage proportion to 40%.

Kỹ năng 2: Inflamed
Hỗ Trợ Đặc Biệt Bị Động

Gains immunity to Controlling Effects and Banish. Reduces healing effects received by 50%. Grants High Spirit's Force to all allies.

  • Lv.2: Reduces the reduction to healing effect received to 40%.
  • Lv.3: High Spirit's Force additionally increases ally's damage reduction by 1% for each 1% HP they lost.
  • Lv.4: Reduces the reduction to healing effect received to 30%.
  • Lv.5: The High Spirit's Force effect is doubled on himself.

High Spirit's Force
[Buff, Mark] Increases damage by 1% for each 1% of HP lost. Demoniac Power effects from multiple Demoniac Shuten Doji cannot be stacked.

Hiệu Ứng Kiểm Soát
Freeze, Sleep, Confuse, Taunt, Silence, Bound, Morph, Daze, Provocation, Deep Freeze, Shadowbound, and Frostbound are controlling effects. The last six cannot be dispelled. Frostbound cannot be removed.

Kỹ năng 3: Raging Blaze
Xua Tan Giảm Tốc Đặc Biệt Lửa: 3

Unleashes a powerful demoniac force and sends out a thundering roar, lowering the Move Bar of all enemies by 30% and dispelling 1 buff from them. Enters the Demoniac State for 2 turns.
The skill can only be used when he has not entered the Demoniac State.

  • Lv.2: Increases dispelled buffs to 2.
  • Lv.3: When his Demoniac State ends, Recover the HP of all allies by the damage he dealt during the state.
  • Lv.4: Increases Move Bar-lowering effect to 40%.
  • Lv.5: When an ally is burned, if their HP drops below 30%, Removes all Controlling Effects from them.

Demoniac State
[Genera|, Mark] Set the world ablaze. An ally has 24% of their current HP burned away at the start of their turn and becomes immune to damage until the start of the next turn. Burning counts as losing HP and can remove Sleep.

Recover
Different from Healing. Unaffected by Heal Down effects. Does not trigger effects related to healing, and cannot land Crits.

Remove
Removes status and effects that cannot be removed.

Hiệu Ứng Kiểm Soát
Freeze, Sleep, Confuse, Taunt, Silence, Bound, Morph, Daze, Provocation, Deep Freeze, Shadowbound, and Frostbound are controlling effects. The last six cannot be dispelled. Frostbound cannot be removed.
