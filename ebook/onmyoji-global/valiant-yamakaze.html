Valiant Yamakaze

Kỹ năng 1: Swift
Sát Thương Lửa: 0

Charges at 1 enemy and launches a 2-strike attack, dealing damage equal to 40% of his ATK with each strike.

  • Lv.2: Tăng sát thương lên 42%.
  • Lv.3: Tăng sát thương lên 44%.
  • Lv.4: Tăng sát thương lên 46%.
  • Lv.5: Tăng sát thương lên 50%.

Kỹ năng 2: Command
Đặc Biệt Bị Động

During battle, each time Briskey gains 1 MP, raises the Move Bar of Valiant Yamakaze by an additional 1%. When a Controlling Effect on Valiant Yamakaze is dispelled or naturally expires, if he's not under any Controlling Effects, then raises his Move Bar by 30% (an additional 1% is raised for each 1 MP Briskey has; limited to 90% Move Bar raised). When Valiant Yamakaze deals damage to an enemy, inflicts an extra 0.5% damage for every 1% of HP the enemy has lost.

  • Lv.2: Reduces his AoE damage taken by 50%.
  • Lv.3: At the start of Valiant Yamakaze's turn, each 1 MP Briskey has amplifies his Crit DMG by 1% when he deals damage.
  • Lv.4: For all allies, each time they take a Crit DMG, Briskey gains 10 MP (triggers only up to once each attack).
  • Lv.5: At the start of battle, Briskey gains 40 MP.

Hiệu Ứng Kiểm Soát
Freeze, Sleep, Confuse, Taunt, Silence, Bound, Morph, Daze, Provocation, Deep Freeze, Shadowbound, and Frostbound are controlling effects. The last six cannot be dispelled. Frostbound cannot be removed.

Briskey
At the start of Valiant Yamakaze's turn, if he's not under any controlling effects, Briskey executes a command from among Briskey's Shelter, Briskey's Hunting Eyes, and Briskey's Blow Off.

Briskey's Shelter Lửa: 0
Gain 40 MP after using the skill.
Briskey hovers behind an ally, creating a shield (cannot be dispelled) lasting 2 turns to protect them, absorbing damage equal to 75% of Valiant Yamakaze's ATK. The shield also grants 100% Crit RES.
Briskey's Hunting Eyes Lửa: 0
Gain 40 MP after using the skill.
Briskey gazes at 1 enemy, reducing their ATK, DEF, and SPD by 35% for 2 turns.
Briskey's Blow Off Lửa: 0
Consume 80 MP after using the skill.
Briskey hovers before 1 enemy, blowing them off the ground. Their Soul-set effects and passive skills are sealed when blown off. They take damage equal to 240% of Valiant Yamakaze's ATK when landing. (Monsters cannot be blown off. They take damage equal to 480% of Valiant Yamakaze's ATK when landing instead. Briskey then gains 40 MP.)

Kỹ năng 3: Storm
Sát Thương Lửa: 3

Rushes towards 1 enemy with a vortex of fire and slashes them 4 times, dealing damage equal to 63% of his ATK each time.

  • Lv.2: Tăng sát thương lên 66%.
  • Lv.3: Tăng sát thương lên 69%.
  • Lv.4: Tăng sát thương lên 72%.
  • Lv.5: Tăng sát thương lên 75%.
