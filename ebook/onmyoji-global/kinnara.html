Kinnara

Kỹ năng 1: Intro
Sát Thương Lửa: 0

Strums her lute to play the prelude and attack 1 enemy, dealing damage equal to 100% of her ATK.

  • Lv.2: Tăng sát thương lên 105%.
  • Lv.3: Tăng sát thương lên 110%.
  • Lv.4: Tăng sát thương lên 115%.
  • Lv.5: Attack inflicts the effects of the last Tune she played.

Kỹ năng 2: Raided
Đặc Biệt Lửa: 1

At the start of her turn, one of the Tunes among Do: Crimson Dusk, Re: Royalty Orange, Mi: Glowing Shrine, So: Gentle Breeze, and La: Purity is available to her. Related effects are triggered when she plays a Tune.

  • Lv.2: After playing a tune, raises her Move Bar by 35%.
  • Lv.3: At the start of a round, gains an effect that reduces her Crit DMG taken by 50%. Each time after playing a tune, converts 10% Crit DMG reduction to increase her Crit DMG by 20%.
  • Lv.4: After playing a tune, gains her Shelter that lasts 1 turn and negates control ling effects once.
  • Lv.5: Reduces the orb cost of playing a tune by 1 orb.
Do: Crimson Dusk Lửa: 1
Deals damage to 1 enemy equal to 150% of her ATK with a 100% base chance of inflicting Isolation on the enemy, lasting 2 turns.
Re: Royalty Orange Lửa: 1
Launches a 5-strike attack on random enemies, dealing damage equal to 90% of her ATK on each strike. The strikes inflicted won't trigger the Soul-set effects of all enemies.
Mi: Glowing Shrine Lửa: 1
Raises the Move Bar of all allies by 10% and increases their Crit DMG by 40% for 2 turns.
So: Gentle Breeze Lửa: 1
Heals the HP of all allies by 80% of her ATK and raises their DEF by 40% for 2 turns.
La: Purity Lửa: 1
Grants you 1 orb and dispels 1 debuff or controlling effect from each ally. If the number of effects dispelled is below 3, you gain an additional orb.

Kỹ năng 3: Conclusion
Sát Thương Lửa: 3

Plays the Tune of Itsukushima and attacks 1 enemy with music notes 5 times, dealing damage equal to 50% of her ATK each time.

  • Lv.2: Tăng sát thương lên 53%.
  • Lv.3: Tăng sát thương lên 56%.
  • Lv.4: Tăng sát thương lên 60%.
  • Lv.5: Attack inflicts the effects of all the Tunes she played.
