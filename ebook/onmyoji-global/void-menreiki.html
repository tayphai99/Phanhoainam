Void Menreiki

Kỹ năng 1: Obliterate Existence
Sát Thương Lửa: 0

Casts a broken mask at 1 enemy, dealing Indirect Damage equal to 100% of her ATK.

  • Lv.2: Tăng sát thương lên 105%.
  • Lv.3: Tăng sát thương lên 110%.
  • Lv.4: Tăng sát thương lên 115%.
  • Lv.5: Tăng sát thương lên 125%.

Indirect Damage
Damage type. Does not trigger soul effects, cannot be shared, and is guaranteed to land critical hits on enemies with 0 DEF.

Kỹ năng 2: Two Faces
Hỗ Trợ Đặc Biệt Lửa: 0 Hiệu Quả Duy Nhất

When a non-summoned entity is KO'd, converges their soul into a Void Mask. When she takes lethal damage, she becomes Darkened.
Active skill: Can use when she has collected at least 4 Void Mask. Ignites the Watchful Eye on the enemy and she becomes Darkened.

  • Lv.2: When she's Darkened, each Void Mask increases the SPD of all allies by 5.
  • Lv.3: Each Void Mask adds a strike to Recurrence Strike.
  • Lv.4: If she hasn't collected any Void Masks until the start of her next turn, she collects a Void Mask at the end of her next turn.
  • Lv.5: Grants a new turn after using the skill

Void Mask
[General, Mark] She can have up to 7 masks. Each mask allows her to ignore DEF by 50 when she attacks.

Darkened
[General, Mark] Removes the Watchful Eye and all debuffs on herself, changing Void Dreamland to Recurrence Strike and recovering her HP by 100%. Also, her attacks ignore DEF by 200 and inflicts 15% HP Steal.

Hiệu Quả Duy Nhất
Nếu có nhiều thức thần cùng tên trong đội hình của bạn, chỉ một trong các kỹ năng của thức thần đó sẽ có hiệu lực.

Kỹ năng 3: Void Dreamland
Sát Thương Tăng Tốc Đặc Biệt Lửa: 3

Opens up a dreamland, inflicts Watchful Eye on 1 enemy and raises the Move Bar of all allies by 25%. If the enemy is already inflicted with Watchful Eye, ignites it before inflicting it on the enemy again.

  • Lv.2: At the start of the turn, mark Rancor equal to 131% of her ATK to the Watchful Eye.
  • Lv.3: 20% of the damage dealt by allies is marked as Rancor in the Watchful Eye.
  • Lv.4: When taking damage, marks 30% of the damage taken as Rancor in the Watchful Eye.
  • Lv.5: When Watchful Eye is inflicted on the same target, there's a 100% Tỷ Lệ Cơ Bản of causing their passive skills to fail until the end of Void Menreiki's next turn.

Watchful Eye
[Genera|, Mark] Exclusive effective. Reduces target's DEF by 20%. Removed after ignited. Ignited automatically after 2 turns.

Tỷ Lệ Cơ Bản
Tỷ lệ bị ảnh hưởng bởi Hiệu Ứng Chính Xác (Effect HIT).

Recurrence Strike Sát Thương Lửa: 3
Attacks 1 enemy, dealing indirect damage equal to 63% of her ATK. If she has strikes left when KO'ing the enemy, changes her attack to the enemy with the lowest HP.
