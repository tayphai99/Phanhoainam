Otakemaru

Kỹ năng 1: Hillcry: Blast
Sát Thương Hiệu Ứng Bất Lợi Lửa: 0

Wields the Jade Blade of Yasakani and unleashes a wave of energy at 1 enemy, dealing damage equal to 100% of his ATK, with a 100% Tỷ Lệ Cơ Bản of reducing the enemy's SPD by 10 for 2 turns. The effect cannot be dispelled.

  • Lv.2: Tăng sát thương lên 105%.
  • Lv.3: Tăng sát thương lên 110%.
  • Lv.4: Tăng sát thương lên 115%.
  • Lv.5: Tăng sát thương lên 125%.

Tỷ Lệ Cơ Bản
Tỷ lệ bị ảnh hưởng bởi Hiệu Ứng Chính Xác (Effect HIT).

Kỹ năng 2: Earth Magic
Phòng Thủ Bị Động

Otakemaru conjures Force of Earth to protect himself at the end of his turn until the start of his next turn.
Force of Earth breaks after taking 2 damages.

  • Lv.2: Increases the damage reduction effect of Force of Earth to 70%.
  • Lv.3: Force of Earth can take an extra attack.
  • Lv.4: Increases the damage reduction effect of Force of Earth to 80%.
  • Lv.5: Upper Hand: Gains Force of Earth

Force of Earth
[Buff, Mark] Reduces damage taken by 60%, up to a maximum damage reduction equal to 40% of his ATK each time.

Upper Hand
Take action at the start of battle.

Kỹ năng 3: Unlimited Blade Prison
Sát Thương Điều Khiển Tăng Tốc Lửa: 4

Uses Unlimited Blade Prison to Banish 1 enemy until the end of Otakemaru's next turn, and conjures a devastating Wave of Rocks to attack all enemies, dealing damage equal to 271% of his ATK. When the Blade Prison closes, the floating rock falls on the Banished enemy, dealing damage equal to 358% of his ATK. The damage cannot be shared, does not trigger the target's Soul effects and Passive skills, and raises the Move Bar of Otakemaru by 40%.
When the Unlimited Blade Prison is active, the skill cannot be used.

  • Lv.2: Increases the damage of Wave of Rocks to 285%.
  • Lv.3: Increases the damage of Wave of Rocks to 299%.
  • Lv.4: Increases the damage of Wave of Rocks to 312%.
  • Lv.5: 12% of the total damage dealt by Wave of Rocks will be added to the damage of Rockfall (to a max of 600% of Otakemaru's ATK).

Unlimited Blade Prison
[Debuff, Mark] Reduces the damage dealt by Wave of Rocks by 10% for each enemy it attacks. Blade Prison ends immediately when Otakemaru or all enemies other than the banished enemy are KO'd.
If there's only 1 enemy when using the skill or if the enemy is immune to controlling effects, Blade Prison cannot be created. Wave of Rocks deals double damage to the enemy instead.

Banish
Banished target is unable to take actions, they cannot be selected, their Move Bar is locked, their passive skills and Souls equipped won't take effect, they are immune to damage, healing effects, buffs and debuffs.
